### <span style="color: #800000;">Asia-Pacific Community Call Returns</span>

This is a reminder that the Asia-Pacific Community call will be happening on Wednesday, May 19 at 8:00 am US Central/Chicago (UTC -5) / 9:00 pm Beijing time. Please join us! ✨\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #800000;">Documentation Standardization Project Continues</span>

You may have seen recent discussions and PRs around standardizing documentation across working groups. We will be continuing the standardization to include:

  * README.md
  * focus-areas/README.md & focus-areas/<focus-area-name>/README.md
  * CONTRIBUTING.md
  * code-of-conduct.md

Huge thanks to Yash and Ritik for their continued work on this project! ✨ You can read Yash&#8217;s post to the mailing list [here.][1]\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #800000;">Reminder: Come Join us in Slack!</span>

CHAOSS has revived our Slack channel as a form of connecting with our community and holding space for newcomers to ask questions. If you like Slack, join us here! <https://join.slack.com/t/chaoss-workspace/shared_invite/zt-ptka3wu9-ey~fd3lh7gj2659WVVzFMQ>. Join our #office-hours channel if you have questions, or just pop in our #general channel to say hello!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Upcoming Workshops for Getting Started as a Contributor to CHAOSS Software</span>

In case you missed it, we have successfully launched a series of free virtual workshops to help you get started contributing to CHAOSS software (mostly centered on Augur). All workshops will be hosted on our Community Zoom (<https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>) and are from 8am &#8211; 10am CDT, 6:30pm &#8211; 8:30pm IST. You can join as an attendee, or a mentor! GSoC Students are also highly encouraged to attend.

  * May 24, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS)][2]
  * June 5, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Restful API Development][3]
  * June 19, 2021 &#8211;  [Jupyter Notebooks: Getting Started][4]
  * July 17, 2021 &#8211; [Jupyter Notebook Visualizations][5]
  * July 31, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS & Intro to Github Workflow)][6]

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
