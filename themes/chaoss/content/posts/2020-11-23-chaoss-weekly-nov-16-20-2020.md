### <span style="color: #980000;">No Meetings Week of November 23</span>

Due to the Thanksgiving Holiday for the US, it was decided in the weekly community meeting that we would cancel all meetings this week. Meetings will resume the week of November 30. See you all then!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSScast Episode 23 &#8211; GSOC and Outreachy Students Aastha Bist and Tola Ore-Aruwaji</span>

We were so happy to host our students Aastha Bist and Tola Ore-Aruwaji this week on CHAOSScast! Tola and Aastha sat down with Matt Snell, Saleh Abdel Motaall and Venia Logan to talk about their contributions to CHAOSS&#8217;s D&I efforts over the last few months. Aastha was instrumental in launching the D&I Badging initiative, and Tola was the key to launching our translation efforts. We are so grateful for all their work, and for being on the show! You can listen here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/23>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Community Handbook Released for Review</span>

Thanks to the herculean efforts of Jaskirat Singh and Xiaoya Xia, our Community Handbook is complete and ready for your review! You can read it in its entirety here: [<span style="font-weight: 400;">https://handbook.chaoss.community/community-handbook/</span>][1]. Issues can be opened up for improvements or suggestions here <https://github.com/chaoss/community-handbook>. Congratulations on shipping this important documentation, Xiaoya and Jaskirat!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Upcoming Meetings </span>

Nope, none this week.
