### <span style="color: #980000">Managing Risk and Opportunities in Open Source on CHAOSScast Episode 10</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  This week we were incredibly honored to have Frank Nagle (Harvard Business School) and Dr. David Wheeler (Linux Foundation) as guests on CHAOSScast. Along with our panelists Kate Stewart, Georg Link, and Sean Goggins, they talked about open source risk management, Software Configuration Management systems, open source licenses and dependencies, and of course, the metrics involved. You&#8217;ll certainly feel smarter after listening to this episode.  <a href="https://podcast.chaoss.community/10">https://podcast.chaoss.community/10</a>.
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">Next Week is the Final Week for Metrics Candidate Reviews</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  If you haven&#8217;t had a chance to review the metrics that are up for release with the August release, you still have time! The deadline for reviewing potential metrics has been extended to July 31, so although your time is running out, you do still have a few days left. If you have a chance to add your input to the issues, it would be incredibly helpful and welcomed. You can see all the metrics up for review here: <a href="https://chaoss.local/metrics/">https://chaoss.local/metrics/</a>
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

<p dir="ltr">
  We are so proud of the hard work coming from our GSOC and Outreachy students! Here are just a few highlights from 2 of our students, and we will continue to highlight different students&#8217; work in our weekly newsletter. They&#8217;re all doing a fantastic job! Read more on their blogs below:

  * [Abhinav&#8217;s working with Merge Request Workers and will be testing them with multiple repositories][2]
