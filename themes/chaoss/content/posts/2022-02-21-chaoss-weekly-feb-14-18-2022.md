### <span style="color: #993300;">Metrics Freeze is Almost Here</span>

In preparation for our April Metrics Release, we are currently scheduled to freeze the metric release candidates on **March 1.** This allows for a 30-day public comment period, where anyone can jump into the conversation and comment on new metrics we&#8217;ve developed. If you&#8217;re taking part in a working group, the agendas should carve out some time to finish up any metrics that are in progress. We&#8217;ll also talk a little about this process in the Community Meeting tomorrow at 11:00 am US Central/Chicago time. You can also ask questions about this in our Slack channel. Join us!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Some Open CfPs for You!</span>

Every now and again we pass along some information around open Calls for Proposals/Presentations/Papers for tech conferences of interest. Of course, we&#8217;d love to have you submit something related to CHAOSS but it&#8217;s completely fine if you don&#8217;t! 😆 We encourage you to submit something anyway. If you&#8217;re a new speaker and you&#8217;d like some feedback on your talk proposal, we have many seasoned speakers who would love to help. Simply post something in the #general channel of our Slack.

  * <span style="font-weight: 400;">OSS Summit North America Call for Presentations: </span>[<span style="font-weight: 400;">https://events.linuxfoundation.org/open-source-summit-north-america/program/cfp/</span>][1] <span style="font-weight: 400;"> </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Deadline March 14, Conference is </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">June 21-24, 2022 in Austin, TX and Virtual </span>
  * Upstream Call for Papers: [https://www.papercall.io/upstream?utm\_medium=email&utm\_content=204168406&utm\_source=hs\_email][2] Deadline April 15, Conference is June 7, Virtual only
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: </span><span style="color: #993300;">GSOC Ideas are Open!</span>

CHAOSS is thrilled to be applying for GSOC once again. This experience has been extremely valuable and rewarding for mentees and mentors alike, and for the overall CHAOSS project. Ideas are currently being collected here: <span style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="https://github.com/chaoss/community/blob/main/GSoC-Ideas.md">https://github.com/chaoss/community/blob/main/GSoC-Ideas.md</a>. You can add your idea by forking this repo and submitting a PR. They have not yet announced accepted participating organizations, but stay tuned. The general GSOC timeline can be found <a href="https://developers.google.com/open-source/gsoc/timeline">here</a>. </span>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Office Hours are Back</span>

This is a reminder that our open Office Hours for newcomers (or anyone, really) happen **every Tuesday from 9:00 am &#8211; 10:00 am US Central/Chicago** **time**. Feel free to join as you wish at any time during that hour at <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>. No registration is needed and you can ask anything you want about the CHAOSS project!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
