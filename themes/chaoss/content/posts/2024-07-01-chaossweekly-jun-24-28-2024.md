### <span style="color: #008080;">Meeting Cancellations Week of July 1-5</span>

July 4 is a US holiday, and as such, we decided at this week&#8217;s CHAOSS Community meeting to take a break from meetings that week!

The following meetings are canceled:

  * Newcomer Hangout
  * CHAOSS Weekly Community Call
  * App Ecosystem Working Group
  * DEI Working Group
  * Data Science Working Group
  * Metrics Models Working Group
  * Metrics Development Working Group
  * CHAOSS Africa Designers Sync
  * CHAOSS Africa Developer meeting

Meetings will resume as usual July 8.
