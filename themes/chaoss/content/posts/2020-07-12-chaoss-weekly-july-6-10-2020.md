### <span style="color: #980000">Review Period Still Open &#8211; Please Review!</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  There have been some excellent comments on our release candidate metrics thus far, but we&#8217;d love even more participation! We encourage you to visit our <a href="https://chaoss.local/metrics/">metrics page</a> and click on each item marked with the &#8220;under review&#8221; label. Even if your comment or reaction is a simple +1, it lets us know that you had a chance to look at them. Any and all feedback is appreciated! <!--more-->
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">Ruth Cheesley On Our Latest CHAOSScast Episode</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  This week we were honored to have Ruth Cheesley as our guest on CHAOSScast Episode #8.  Ruth shares her journey around community management in the Mautic community, and her community health dashboard. We also discuss the issues of privacy and defining other contributions aside from coding. It&#8217;s really great stuff. You can listen here: <a href="https://podcast.chaoss.community/8">https://podcast.chaoss.community/8</a>.
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">D&I Badging Pilot Program Seeking Volunteers</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  This is a very exciting time for our D&I Badging Program, as the Pilot and testing period will begin next week! From July 13-17, volunteers will be going through the process from start to finish as &#8220;applicants&#8221; or &#8220;reviewers,&#8221; in order to test the process before the official launch. If you would like to be part of this process, it is not super time consuming and would be really helpful to the team. Please reach out to Matt Snell or join their <a href="https://chaoss-workspace.slack.com/join/shared_invite/zt-dqeab4ab-4XrH51rc4y_WXjN~uI~6rA#/">Slack channel</a> for more information on how to lend a hand.
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
