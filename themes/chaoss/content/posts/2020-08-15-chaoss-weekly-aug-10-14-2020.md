### <span style="color: #980000">Volunteers Needed for D&I Badging Phase 2 </span>

<p dir="ltr">
  Maybe you&#8217;ve always wanted to be an event organizer, but thought &#8220;nah, that&#8217;s a lot of work.&#8221; (You&#8217;re not wrong, it <em>is</em> a lot of work). The good news is now you can have all the glory that comes with being an event organizer without any of the actual work! <a href="https://lists.linuxfoundation.org/pipermail/chaoss-diversity-inclusion/2020-August/000261.html">Our D&I Badging Program is searching for volunteers to submit, review, and moderate mock events for Phase 2 of their pilot testing</a>. Check the post and reach out if you have a few extra minutes to spare for this excellent cause.
</p>

<p dir="ltr">

</p>

### <span style="color: #980000">Gina Helfrich on CHAOSScast Episode 13: Internews OSS Lightweight Needs Assessment Toolkit</span>

The amazing Gina Helfrich was our guest for this week&#8217;s CHAOSScast. Gina talks about her work with the BASICS project (Building Analytical and Support Infrastructure for Critical Security tools) and her journey with open source metrics (including CHAOSS). This was such an excellent podcast! Listen here: [https://podcast.chaoss.community/13.][1]\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">Google Summer of Code and Outreachy Student Highlights</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  With only a few weeks left in the summer mentorship programs, our students have made excellent progress.  Here are just a few highlights from 2 of our students, and we will continue to highlight different students&#8217; work in our weekly newsletter. They&#8217;re all doing a fantastic job! Read more on their blogs below:
</p>

  * [Venu completed the pilot study for analyzing metrics on Gitlab, and plans to expand that work into GitHub, using CHAOSS as the pilot study for that implementation][2].
  * [Ria continues to evolve the implementation of SCMS inside CHAOSS, with continuous upgrades to its UX and functionality, and assistance with tagging and calibration of the codex. ][3]

Keep up the awesome work!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">Our Latest Metrics Were Officially Released, Hooray!</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  In case you missed it, we officially announced the shipping of our latest metrics release on our <a href="https://chaoss.local/blog-post/2020/08/11/2020-08-metrics-release/">blog</a>, <a href="https://lists.linuxfoundation.org/pipermail/chaoss/2020-August/001769.html">mailing list</a>, and <a href="https://twitter.com/CHAOSSproj/status/1293254205310992384">Twitter</a>. We&#8217;d love your help spreading the word and encouraging people to read about and use some of our metrics, so feel free to spread the news to from the platforms of your choice. 🙂
</p>
