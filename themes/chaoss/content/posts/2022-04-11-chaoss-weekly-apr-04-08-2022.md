### <span style="color: #993300;">New CHAOSScast Released: Episode 56 &#8211; Using GrimoireLab to Understand the Health of Your Open Source Project</span>

On this week&#8217;s episode of CHAOSScast, we are joined by CHAOSS community member Chenqi Shan from Huawei. Chenqi chatted with Daniel Izquierdo, Sean Goggins, and Venia Logan to discuss her work with building metrics models for the open source community openEuler. They talk about which metrics might be more useful for open source projects at varying stages of the project&#8217;s lifecycle, using OpenSearch, and the different components of GrimoireLab. We are thrilled to have Chenqi in the CHAOSS community and you can listen to this episode here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/56>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder for our Outreachy Applicants</span>

CHAOSS is very excited to be participating in Outreachy again this year, and we&#8217;d like to extend a warm welcome to all those who have recently joined the community! Here&#8217;s a reminder that we have a few resources available to you:

  * the [#outreachy Slack channel][2] for specific questions on Outreachy projects and for connecting with other Outreachy applicants
  * the [CHAOSS Community Handbook][3]
  * the weekly Community Meeting and Open Office Hours (details on joining those calls are below)

When you&#8217;d like to register your interest officially, you can [add your name to this document][4] via a PR. **The deadline to apply for an Outreachy internship is April 22, 2022**. Welcome, welcome new friends!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder for our Google Summer of Code Applicants</span>

For our GSOC applicants, we are also extremely excited to be participating in the GSOC cohort once again! Here are some resources for you, as you learn about CHAOSS and join the community.

  * the [#gsoc-2022][5] Slack channel for specific questions on GSOC projects and for connecting with other GSOC applicants
  * the [CHAOSS Community Handbook][3]
  * the weekly Community Meeting and Open Office Hours (details on joining those calls are below)

When you&#8217;d like to register your interest officially, you can [add your name to this document][6] via a PR. **The deadline to apply for the GSOC program is April 19, 2022**. Best of luck and welcome to CHAOSS!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSS App Ecosystem Meetings on Hiatus</span>

The CHAOSS App Ecosystem group is taking a break, so all upcoming meetings are canceled. This group will be reorganizing and relaunching at some point in the future. Thanks to all who have been participating in these meetings!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Metrics Are Being Finalized</span>

We have an upcoming release for our new metrics (2022-04) so we are in the final stages of adding them to the release. During this process, there will be no PRs merged in for new metrics or revisions to the current set of metrics. Working Groups are asked to make sure all metrics are in the state they should be, and that they are all added to the translations repository. Thanks for your help!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
