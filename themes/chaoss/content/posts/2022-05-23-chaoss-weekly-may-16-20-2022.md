### <span style="color: #993300;">Say Hello to the CHAOSS Welcome Slack Bot &#8211; A She Code Africa Project</span>

Thanks to the amazing efforts of Precious Abubakar and Iyimide Adegunloye from the She Code Africa mentorship program, we have a new friend in the [#newcomers Slack channel][1]! This new friend is a welcome bot that will respond privately to anyone who types the word &#8220;newbie&#8221; in the channel. From there they can get answers to some of our common questions about getting started in the CHAOSS community. (Go ahead and try it out!)

What&#8217;s also great about this bot is that we can take this foundation and add information to it as the CHAOSS community grows and evolves. We are incredibly grateful to Precious and Mide and the wonderful work they&#8217;ve done to improve the onboarding experience for newcomers to the community. Congratulations on completing a very successful mentorship program! (And a big thank you to their mentors Ruth Ikegah, Matt Cantu Snell, and Elizabeth Barron).\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSScon EU 2022 CfP Closes Next Week! </span>

Here&#8217;s your reminder that you have one more week to [submit something to CHAOSScon EU 2022][12]. We are accepting proposals for a variety of delivery formats.

All submissions should relate to the CHAOSS project&#8217;s mission of developing metrics, methodologies, and software for expressing open source project health and sustainability. By measuring open source project health and sustainability, CHAOSS seeks to improve the transparency and actionability of open source project health and sustainability so that relevant stakeholders can make more informed decisions about open source project engagement.

### Software related metrics topics:

  * tutorials
  * walk throughs
  * demos
  * how-to&#8217;s
  * new features

### Metrics related topics:

  * metrics strategy
  * deep dive into the use of metrics
  * failure of metrics in practice
  * lessons learned, use cases, and case studies
  * proposals for new metrics
  * new visualizations

For more information (or to register) visit the [CHAOSScon EU page][13] on the CHAOSS website.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
