### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: You&#8217;re Invited! DEI Event Badging Reviewer Appreciation Event June 16</span>

Please join us on **June 16, 2022 at 8:00 am US Central/Chicago time** ([convert to your time][1]) as we celebrate our amazing team of reviewers and the success of the DEI Badging Initiative! This will be a fun, informal virtual event, and of course, there is no cost to participate. You can [register for the event here][2].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### **Reminder: Open Source Summit Europe Deadline: Monday, May 30, 2022 at 11:59 PM PDT**

Please let us know if you submit a CHAOSS-related topic!

### **News!: CHAOSS Software Workshops, Tutorials and Hackathons at Open Source Summit Europe: DETAILS FORTHCOMING!**

### **Volunteer Opportunity: Help With Our Maintainer Burnout Session **

As part of GitHub&#8217;s Maintainer Month (June), CHAOSS is hosting a one-hour interactive workshop on Maintainer Burnout. This is in the early stages of planning, but you can find more information in the [initial planning document][3]. We are currently working toward confirming a date and time, and the agenda. If you would like to help with this event, please reach out to Ruth Ikegah on the CHAOSS slack.

### **CHAOSS Meeting Changes**

There are 2 meeting updates to be aware of:

<li aria-level="1">
  REMINDER: The App Ecosystem Meeting is switching day and time to <b>every other Tuesday at 1:00 pm US Central/Chicago time, starting May 10</b>.
</li>

As always, these changes are reflected on the main CHAOSS calendar which you can subscribe to on the [CHAOSS Participate page][4].

### **CHAOSS Web Content Meeting Revival**

We are reviving the Web Content meetings, hooray! The purpose of these meetings in the short term is to discuss the redesign of the CHAOSS website. If you would like to be part of this project, you can join these meetings &#8212; they are open to everyone.

These meetings will happen **every other Thursday at 9:00 am US Central/Chicago time, starting on May 12**.

### **Reminder: You&#8217;re Invited! DEI Event Badging Reviewer Appreciation Event June 16**

This will be a fun, informal virtual event, and of course, there is no cost to participate. You can [register for the event here][2].
