### <span style="color: #980000;">Free Tutorial Workshops for Getting Started as a Contributor to CHAOSS Software</span>

If you&#8217;ve ever wanted to contribute code to CHAOSS Software, but weren&#8217;t sure how to get started, this series of free virtual workshops are for you! All workshops will be hosted on our Community Zoom (<https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>) and are from 8am &#8211; 10am CDT, 6:30pm &#8211; 8:30pm IST. You can join as an attendee, or a mentor! GSoC Students are also highly encouraged to attend.

  * April 6, 2021 &#8211;  [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS & Intro to Github Workflow)][1]
  * April 9, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS & Intro to Github Workflow)][2]
  * April 13, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Restful API Development][3]
  * April 20, 2021 &#8211;  [Jupyter Notebooks: Getting Started][4]
  * April 27, 2021 &#8211; [Jupyter Notebook Visualizations][5]
  * May 24, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS)][6]
  * June 5, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Restful API Development][7]
  * June 19, 2021 &#8211;  [Jupyter Notebooks: Getting Started][8]
  * July 17, 2021 &#8211; [Jupyter Notebook Visualizations][9]
  * July 31, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS & Intro to Github Workflow)][10]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Reminder: App Ecosystem Working Group Meets Today</span>

Because of the awkward timing of when the newsletter usually goes out, the App Ecosystem Working Group never makes it onto our list of Upcoming Meetings. 😭 This is a super interesting group that works on developing metrics pertinent to the larger open source ecosystem. Regular participants of this group come from a variety of communities and the conversation is always lively. For recent examples of their work, check out this blog post on opensource.com ([How We Develop Success Metrics for Open Source Events][11]) and the recent CHAOSScast episode ([Marketing Metrics for OSS Foundations][12]).

This group meets every other Monday at noon US Central (Chicago) time on our Community Zoom (<https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>), with the next meeting happening TODAY! Please feel free to join!

(And apologies to the regulars in this group &#8211; we ❤️ you!)\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
