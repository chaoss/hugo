For those who aren&#8217;t familiar with the event, [Hacktoberfest][1] is a month-long initiative to encourage open source participation from new contributors. It&#8217;s sponsored by Digital Ocean and Major League Hacking, and it includes [open source events, swag, and recognition for open source contributions][2]. 

CHAOSS is participating in a few ways this year.

### From Zero to 100 Technical Writing Workshop {.wp-block-heading}

Our CHAOSS Africa Technical Writing group is hosting a 3 hour workshop on technical documentation and how writers can get involved in open source. It will happen on **October 18 from 2:00-5:00 pm UTC**, and you can register for the event [here][3]. You can also read more about the event in the [CHAOSS Africa Repo][4]. 

### X Space on Design and Open Source {.wp-block-heading}

On **October 17, 2025** the [CHAOSS Africa X account][5] will be hosting an X Space on Open Source and Design. We encourage anyone interested in learning about the overlap of these two areas to join the Space! You should follow the [CHAOSS Africa X account][5] or the [#hacktoberfest-2025 Slack Channel][6] for up-to-date information. 

### Other Opportunities {.wp-block-heading}

As more opportunities arise, we will be posting them in the [#hacktoberfest-2025 Slack channel][6] in the CHAOSS Slack workspace. If you&#8217;d like to connect with others participating in our Hacktoberfest activities, this is a great place to start! 

Hope to see you in CHAOSS, and happy hacking!
