### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSScast Episode 29: Supporting Teams to Make Data-Driven Decisions with Stefka Dimitrova and Ivana Atanasova</span>

This week we were thrilled to host Stefka Dimitrova and Ivana Atanasova from VMWare. They shared with us their experiences with analyzing open source communities, contributing to the CHAOSS project through Augur, and much more. Kate Stewart, Matt Germonprez, and Georg Link had a wonderful time chatting with Stefka and Ivana, and you can listen in here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/29>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Final Metrics Revisions Due This Week</span>

To all those participating in our Working Groups, this is your last week to take the feedback and comments from the public community and make any changes or revisions to the metrics as needed. On Friday, we will be releasing the metrics and closing the book on the 202103 Release. Exciting times!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">No New Volunteer Opportunity This Week</span>

Huge thank you to all who have reached out about volunteering and helping the CHAOSS project! Stay tuned as we solidify new opportunities for volunteering. 🙂 You are all wonderful and we are so appreciative of your efforts in helping the project move forward!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
