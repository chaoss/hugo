### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSS Meetup Shanghai December 27</span>

Our Asia-Pacific community is hosting a meetup/workshop on December 27 in Shanghai. It will be a mix of pre-recorded video talks and breakout sessions for in-person discussion. It&#8217;s going to be an awesome event, and we are very excited about this! You can learn more here: <{{ baseURL }}shanghai-meetup-2020/>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">New &#8220;Intro to CHAOSS&#8221; Video Available</span>

If you&#8217;re looking for something to pass along to anyone asking what the CHAOSS project is all about, we now have something you can use! This 5 minute video gives a quick summary of all things CHAOSS: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vI5fdMzxNjk>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSScast Episode 25: GSOC with Abhinav Bajpai</span>

This week on CHAOSScast, Sean Goggins and Georg Link chatted with Abhinav Bajpai, one of our Google Summer of Code students, about his work with Gitlab Data, Augur, and his overall experience in the program and with his mentors. Abhinav did great work this year and we were delighted to have him on the podcast! You can listen here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/25>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Reminder: Evolution, Risk, Augur Meeting Changes for 2021</span>

<div id="day-20201201" class="day ">
  <div class="date-label">
    The Evolution, Risk, and Augur working groups have decided to shift their meeting times, starting in 2021.
  </div>
  
  <ul>
    <li>
      Risk WG will be 10-10:50 am Central (Chicago) Time every other Tuesday, <strong>starting January 5</strong>.
    </li>
    <li>
      Evolution WG will be 11:00-11:50 am Central (Chicago) Time every other Wednesday, <strong>starting January 6</strong>.
    </li>
    <li>
      Augur will be 11:00-11:50 am Central (Chicago) Time every other Thursday, <strong>starting January 7.</strong>
    </li>
  </ul>
  
  <p>
    We will continue to list the upcoming meetings in this newsletter and through the website here: <a href="{{ baseURL }}participate/">{{ baseURL }}participate/</a>.
  </p>
</div>
