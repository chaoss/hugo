### <span style="color: #993300;">No Meetings This Week</span>

There will be no meetings this week, because of OSSummit and CHAOSScon. We will see you back again next week!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSScon Livestreamed for Free (Yes, Really!)</span>

We will be broadcasting CHAOSScon on our [Youtube channel][1], for free! Tune in Thursday, September 30 from 9:00 am &#8211; 12:30 pm US Pacific Time, and join the CHAOSS group. You can also join the [#chaosscon channel][2] on our [Community Slack][3]

Some [highlights from our schedule][4] include:

  * Keynote presentation from **Emma Irwin, Microsoft**
  * **6 talks** from industry, academia, researchers, and the CHAOSS community
  * On-site signup for **Lightning Talk** sessions

We also plan on doing an informal CHAOSS meetup while we are in Seattle, but that is still a work in progress. Keep your eyes on Slack and Twitter if you&#8217;re interested in joining.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">3 More Days to Review Metrics</span>

Our 30-day public review process for the metrics candidates is almost over, so you have just a few more days to take a look! **PLEASE** feel free to add your input, +1&#8217;s, or any other comments to any or all of these **16 metrics**:

  * [Programming Language Distribution][5]
  * [Collaboration Platform Activity][6]
  * [Technical Fork (re-opened)][7]
  * [Clones][8]
  * [Bot Activity][9]
  * [Event Locations][10]
  * [Psychological Safety][11]
  * [Inclusive Experience at Event][12]
  * [Event Demographics][13]
  * [Time Inclusion for Virtual Events][14]
  * [Documentation Discoverability][15]
  * [Organizational Influence][16]
  * [Contribution Attribution][17]
  * [Libyear][18]
  * [Upstream Dependency Count][19]
  * [Academic Open Source Project Impact][20]

Thank you so much for your help in making our metrics the best they can be.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
