### <span style="color: #993300;">Important Meeting Changes</span>

A new year often brings the opportunity for working groups to reassess their meeting days and times, to make sure they are still convenient for those who enjoy participating in them. The following changes are happening on the CHAOSS calendar:

  * Starting February 9, **Evolution Working Group** will move to every other Thursday at 12:00 pm US Central/Chicago
  * **Risk Working Group** is shifting by a week to offset the OSPO Working Group schedule. They will still happen every other Thursday at 1:00 pm. The next meeting is on February 16.
  * February 2 all CHAOSS meetings have been canceled for CHAOSScon. This includes **Communications, Common, and Web Content Meetings.**

As always you can see the most updated [CHAOSS calendar on our website][1].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reasons to Join us for CHAOSScon EU 2023</span>

We are super excited to invite you to CHAOSScon EU 2023, happening in conjunction with FOSDEM in Brussels, Belgium.  CHAOSScon day will happen on February 3 from 8:00 to 17:00 Central European Standard Time. Did you know:

  * Morning sessions will consist of interactive chats and panel discussions
  * The afternoon will be dedicated to GrimoireLab and Augur
  * During lunch there is an optional OSPO++ session titled [Measuring OSS in Universities][2]
  * The event is not able to be livestreamed, so you&#8217;ll have to join in person!
  * <strong style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen-Sans, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">Registration is open</strong> <span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen-Sans, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">and is $10USD to attend</span>

<span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen-Sans, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">You can learn more about this conference on the </span><a style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen-Sans, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;" href="https://chaoss.local/chaosscon-2023-eu/">CHAOSScon EU 2023 website. </a>

Hope you can join us!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">New CHAOSScast: Revisiting Metrics Journey with Chris &#8220;Mercer&#8221; Mercer</span>

We were thrilled to welcome Chris &#8220;Mercer&#8221; Mercer back to CHAOSScast for another episode! Our panelists Venia Logan and Lori Goldman chat with Mercer about measurement marketing, the ACE model, Google Analytics, and how these can be applied to an open source project. Of particular interest is the discussion on a potential new team at companies and projects specifically around measurement. You won&#8217;t want to miss this episode! You can listen here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/70>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Next Onboarding Session for New Chaotics</span>

The CHAOSS community continues to grow! To make the onboarding process a little easier, Elizabeth Barron (CHAOSS Community Manager) and Ruth Ikegah (CHAOSS Africa Community Lead) will be hosting the next Onboarding Session on February 1, 2023 at 11:00 am US Central/Chicago time**.** We know this time is not convenient for everyone, but this is just a start and we will add and iterate as we go.

This meeting will cover:

  * General overview of CHAOSS
  * How you can begin contributing
  * How to get started with using the metrics
  * Q&A

We hope you can join to meet other newcomers and learn about CHAOSS! You can [convert to your time zone here][3].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">The Last Newsletter Via the Mailing List</span>

Typically when we publish our newsletter, we publish on the website (which triggers a Twitter post and a post in Slack), and then we send it to the CHAOSS mailing list. This will be the last edition to go to the mailing list, and we will be switching to Discourse next week! We plan to launch Discourse on Feb 1 so stay tuned to hear more about that transition.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
