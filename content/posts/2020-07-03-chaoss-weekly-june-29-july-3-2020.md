### <span style="color: #980000">Review Period for Next Metrics Release Candidates Has Begun &#8211; Please Review!</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  After months of hard work and preparation, we have released the next set of metrics releases for the public review period. We would love your help in reviewing the current candidates and we encourage you to visit our <a href="{{ baseURL }}metrics/">metrics page</a> and click on each item marked with the &#8220;under review&#8221; label. Any and all feedback is appreciated!
</p>

### <span style="color: #980000">Latest CHAOSScast with Jono Bacon</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  You won&#8217;t want to miss the latest CHAOSScast! Episode #7 features well known community manager Jono Bacon. With years of experience, Jono offers us valuable insight and advice, and he is a wonderful storyteller. This week we talked about data, the Community Participation Lifecycle. and Earl &#8220;Madman&#8221; Muntz, the father of Muntzing. You can listen here: <a href="https://podcast.chaoss.community/7">https://podcast.chaoss.community/7</a>.
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">OSSNA Wrap-Up</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  Another year of OSSNA has been shipped and is in the books. For those who presented, led BoFs, or participated in panel discussions, we are grateful for your representation of the CHAOSS project and for helping educate the world on what we&#8217;re all about. And for those who attended, we hope you&#8217;re recovering nicely and that you learned something new. It was an intensive week! Recordings will be uploaded to <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/TheLinuxFoundation">The Linux Foundation&#8217;s Youtube channel</a> in a few days, so subscribe for notifications if you&#8217;re interested in reliving the magic. 🌟
</p>
