### <span style="color: #980000">CHAOSScast Episode 11: Diversity and Inclusion with the Fabulous Emma Irwin</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  Creating diverse and inclusive communities is something Emma Irwin has spent years working on at Mozilla. We are so grateful to have a chance to chat with her this week. We discussed our D&I Badging program, avoiding common community health metrics mistakes, and Emma&#8217;s journey in the D&I space. Emma has so much expertise and advice to offer; you will not want to miss this episode! https://podcast.chaoss.community/11
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">OpenStack&#8217;s Open Infrastructure Summit CfP Open for 2 More Days</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  Our friends at OpenStack wanted to let us know that their Call for Papers for the 2020 Open Infrastructure Summit is open until August 5. If you are thinking of submitting something, you still have a little time. This event will be virtual, so you don&#8217;t even need to pack your luggage or even put on shoes to attend. All the info you need to submit is right here: https://cfp.openstack.org/ Good luck!
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">Google Summer of Code and Outreachy Student Highlights</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  We are so proud of the hard work coming from our GSOC and Outreachy students! Here are just a few highlights from 2 of our students, and we will continue to highlight different students&#8217; work in our weekly newsletter. They&#8217;re all doing a fantastic job! Read more on their blogs below:
</p>

  * [Tola&#8217;s been thoroughly vetting and testing four different translation and localization apps so that we may offer our D&I Badging program in languages besides English][1]
  * [Akshara is making awesome progress on analyzing sentiment in messages posted in repos. ][2]
