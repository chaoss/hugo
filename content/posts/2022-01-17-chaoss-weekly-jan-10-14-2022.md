### <span style="color: #993300;">Changes to Office Hours</span>

Our open Office Hours for newcomers will reconvene on **Tuesday, February 1** and will happen **every Tuesday from 9:00 am &#8211; 10:00 am US Central/Chicago** **time**. Feel free to join as you wish at any time during that hour at <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>. No registration is needed and you can ask anything you want about the CHAOSS project!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSScast Episode 50: Recognizing all Kinds of Labor in Open Source Ecosystems with Amanda, Katie, and John</span>

This week, we join Amanda Casari (Google), Katie McLaughlin (Google), and John Meluso (University of Vermont) to discuss Project OCEAN (Open-Source Complex Ecosystems And Networks) and the challenges and strategies around making sure all open source contributions to a project are acknowledged and counted. They also discuss our Types of Contributions Metric, and the complexities of the open source ecosystem. Along with Amanda, Katie, and John, join Georg Link, Matt Germonprez, and Sophia Vargas in one of our most interesting episodes yet. You can listen here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/50>. (Also it&#8217;s our 50th episode so a big shout-out to our CHAOSScast team! Hooray! 🎉)\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Help Give Us a Name</span>

Many communities give themselves a collective name to help define a collective identity and build unity among the community members. The CHAOSS community decided to do just that at the last community meeting. We opened a poll to help us figure out what our name should be, and we&#8217;d love love love your input!

Please help us decide by voting in this sliding poll here: <https://voteupapp.com/shared/lCHNiOfnO>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Welcome Newcomers!</span>

We&#8217;ve seen a lot of new faces and names recently, so we just want to welcome you to the CHAOSS Community. We have a handy form that lets us get to know you a little more, so we can help point you in the right direction when getting started in CHAOSS. If you&#8217;re new to the community and you haven&#8217;t yet filled this form out, we&#8217;d love it if you could do that [here][11].

We also highly recommend you attend one of our CHAOSS Community Weekly Meetings, happening every Tuesday at 11:00 am US Central/Chicago time. If you can&#8217;t make that, you can see [previous recordings of our meetings on CHAOSStube][12]. You can also join our open Office Hours starting in February, happening every Tuesday at 9:00 am US Central/Chicago time.

Reach out to us [on Slack][13] or [via email][14] if you have any questions!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
