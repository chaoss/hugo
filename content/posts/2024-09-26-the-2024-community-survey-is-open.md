We have opened the </span>[<span style="font-weight: 400;">2024 CHAOSS Community Survey</span>][1]<span style="font-weight: 400;">! </span>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">In 2022, </span>[<span style="font-weight: 400;">CHAOSS conducted a survey</span>][2] <span style="font-weight: 400;">to gain a deeper understanding of our community concerning diversity, equity, inclusion, welcomingness, and belonging. The</span>[ <span style="font-weight: 400;">insights we gained</span>][3] <span style="font-weight: 400;">were incredibly beneficial, as DEI is something we continually strive to center and prioritize.</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">Much has changed in the past two years, so it’s time to revisit this survey. As before, we will use the results to get a sense of our community members’ experiences within CHAOSS, to identify any areas for improvement, and to gauge where we are on track.</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">If you are a CHAOTIC (either past or present), we highly encourage you to</span>[ <span style="font-weight: 400;">complete this survey</span>][1]<span style="font-weight: 400;">! Even if you are brand new to the community, we’d love to hear from you. In this way, you can help make CHAOSS better for everyone.</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">This survey:</span>

<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">has 14 questions in 3 sections</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">should take around 10-15 minutes to complete</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">is completely anonymous and no personally identifiable information will be collected</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">is GDPR compliant</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="1">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">will be </span><b>open until October 31</b>
</li>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">Let me stress that I am the only person with access to the raw data, and the scrubbed data will be available to a few CHAOTICS that will help us identify common themes. You are highly encouraged to be as candid as you like; we genuinely want your honest feedback.</span>

[<span style="font-weight: 400;">Click here to take the 2024 CHAOSS Community Survey.</span>][1]

Thank you for your participation and for helping us center diversity, equity, and inclusion in the CHAOSS community!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[/vc\_column\][/vc\_row]
