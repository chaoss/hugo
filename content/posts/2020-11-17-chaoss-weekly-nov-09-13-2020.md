### <span style="color: #980000;">New Community Report Outreach Group</span>

Now that we have the [Community Report initiative][1] smoothly flowing, it&#8217;s time to ramp up our outreach efforts in earnest. If you would like to help participate in putting together and implementing an outreach plan, we&#8217;d love to have you on the team! Weekly meetings have been added to the CHAOSS calendar (Tuesdays at 9:30 am CST, starting November 24) and there is a [spreadsheet][2] of potential projects to reach out to. Join us!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Documentation Projects Are Forging Ahead</span>

Our Google Season of Docs students (Jaskirat Singh and Xiaoya Xia) are making tremendous strides in building out the Community Handbook and the D&I Badging guidelines. Here are their updates:

  * Xiaoya has released the first full edition of the D&I Badging initiative, which you can read and provide feedback on here: <https://handbook.chaoss.community/community-handbook/badging/overview>
  * Jaskirat added a complete History and Evolution of the CHAOSS project, including a beautiful timeline, to the Community Handbook here: <https://app.gitbook.com/@chaoss-project/s/community-handbook/about/chaoss-history>

We are so proud of you both and the incredible contribution you&#8217;re making to the CHAOSS project!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
