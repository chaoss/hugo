### <span style="color: #980000">State of the Source Summit CHAOSS Talks</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  This coming week marks the OSI&#8217;s State of the Source Summit. We are proud to say that some of our CHAOSS contributors will be speaking at this conference:
</p>

  * Georg Link: &#8220;Metrics for open source in light of insights from the CHAOSS Project&#8221;
  * Ruth Ikegah: &#8220;A Beginner-Inclusive Approach to Open Source&#8221;
  * Matt Broberg: &#8220;The next generation of contributors is not on IRC&#8221;
  * Jose Manrique Lopez: &#8220;Open Source Program Office to help on open source sustainability&#8221;

You can see the full schedule here: https://eventyay.com/e/8fa7fd14/schedule, or register for the event here: https://eventyay.com/e/8fa7fd14. (Tickets are free, but you can donate if you feel like it.) Best of luck to all our speakers!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">D&I Badging Reviewer Training Next Week</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  It&#8217;s almost here! The D&I Badging program will be officially released on September 14, and Matt Snell, Aastha Bist, and the rest of the D&I Badging team are gearing up. This week marks three training sessions for those who have expressed interest in becoming a badge reviewer. If you are still interested in this, but haven&#8217;t yet reached out, you still have time! Email <a href="mailto:abist119@gmail.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">abist119@gmail.com</a> or <a href="mailto:msnell@unomaha.edu" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">msnell@unomaha.edu</a> and they can get you pointed in the right direction.
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">CHAOSScast Episode 16: Role Diversity with Silona Bonewald</span>

We were super excited to host Silona Bonewald this week on CHAOSScast! Silona has years of experience with inner source and open source, and shares her insights around the role that metrics play in building successful projects and teams. You won&#8217;t want to miss this one! https://podcast.chaoss.community/16
