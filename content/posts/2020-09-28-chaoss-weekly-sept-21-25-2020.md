### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSScast Episode 19: GSOC: Anomaly Detection with Pratik, Akshara, Sarit, and Tianyi</span>

This week is part of a new series we will be doing, where we interview our students that have participated in any of our mentorship programs. We were really happy to host Pratik Mishra, Sarit Adhikari, Akshara P, and Tianyi Zhou who were all working on anomaly detection. It&#8217;s a great episode and you can listen here! <https://podcast.chaoss.community/19>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSScast Switching to Bi-Weekly Schedule</span>

CHAOSScast is now on a bi-weekly schedule, because of the cost involved with editing, and hosting the podcast. We are on the lookout for anyone who might be interested in sponsoring! If you or your company would like more information, please reach out to Georg Link (georg@chaoss.community) for a sponsorship prospectus. It&#8217;s for an awesome cause!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Hacktoberfest Participation</span>

If you or someone you know has been looking for projects to contribute to during Hacktoberfest, look no further than our very own GrimoireLab and Augur! Both projects will have issues marked for Hacktoberfest participants and would welcome contributions of all kinds!

  * [GrimoireLab][1]
  * [Augur][2]

For more information about Hacktoberfest and what it&#8217;s all about, visit <https://hacktoberfest.digitalocean.com/>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">GSOD Updates</span>

We have two students currently working with us through the Google Season of Docs program: Jaskirat Singh and Xiaoya Xia. They are doing amazing work! Here are their most recent updates:

  * [Jaskirat has been working on documenting our student mentorship programs in our Community Handbook][3]
  * [Xiaoya has been working on the overview for the D&I Badging Program][4]
