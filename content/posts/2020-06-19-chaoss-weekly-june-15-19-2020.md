### <span style="color: #980000">Latest CHAOSScast with Don Marti</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  Episode #5 of CHAOSScast is live! This week we chat with Don Marti about OpenSSL, sustainability versus community health, defining what &#8220;community health&#8221; means to different types of users and contributors, and how metrics can provide insight into the health of a community. And also, neon tetras, picnic backpacks and slackbots! Listen here: https://podcast.chaoss.community/5
</p>

### <span style="color: #980000">CHAOSS Stays with GitHub</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  In case you missed the discussion about potentially migrating the CHAOSS project to GitLab from GitHub, after several months of deliberation and discussion, the decision was made to stay with GitHub for now. You can read the history and summaries of the conversations in this <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1a5_mRWmWBE0yRm8lgBi0myEW7YwfRSgF9pQGR1_IPK0/edit?pli=1#heading=h.zh5jrsxyxlxa">Google Doc.</a>
