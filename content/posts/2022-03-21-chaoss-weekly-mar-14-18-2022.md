### <span style="color: #993300;">Latest Augur Release &#8211; Improved Docs, Processing Time, and More!</span>

The Augur team is happy to announce the latest improvements to the Augur software! Version 0.25.9 includes some awesome updates:

  * [Documentation][1] has been updated
  * The Augur website has been updated, and includes [live examples][2]
  * Commit counting on large installations now 2-12x faster!

You can grab the [latest release of Augur][3], or [see exactly what&#8217;s changed][4]. Keep up the awesome work, Sean and the Augur team!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Metrics Review Period is Open</span>

Now is the time for you to offer last-minute feedback on our metrics candidates prior to their release in April. You don&#8217;t have to have worked on metrics, or participated in the CHAOSS Community before; we value feedback from everyone. This release will include 8 new metrics and 1 metric that will be moving to a metrics model.

  * Occasional Contributors &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-occasional-contributors/> ([Issue for feedback][6])
  * Event Accessibility &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-event-accessibility/> ([Issue for feedback][7])
  * Project Demographics &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-project-demographics/> ([Issue for feedback][8])
  * Change Request Commits &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-change-request-commits/> ([Issue for feedback][9])
  * Change Request Reviews &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-change-request-reviews/> ([Issue for feedback][10])
  * Conversion Rate &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-conversion-rate>/ ([Issue for feedback][11])
  * Defect Resolution Time &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-defect-resolution-time/> ([Issue for feedback][12])

### <span style="color: #993300;">Working Group Spotlight: Evolution </span>

This week in the [Weekly Community meeting][14] we heard an update on our [Evolution Working Group][15].

This group would love for you to attend! They recently added 3 new candidates to our growing list of developed metrics:

  * [Change Request Commits][16] (How many code change commits are included in a change request?)
  * [Change Request Reviews][17] (To what extent are change requests put through a formal review process using platform features?)
  * [Conversion Rate][18] (What are the rates at which new contributors become more sustained contributors?)

Next up for this group is to see what types of metrics are needed for the metrics models that are currently being discussed. These metrics will be crucial in helping our users implement our models in the real world.

Sean Goggins is the lead for this Working Group, so feel free to reach out or join the [Evolution Working Group slack channel][19]. The details for this meeting are:

  * They meet every other Tuesday at 10:00 am US Central/Chicago time
  * Next meeting is tomorrow, March 22
  * Agenda and minutes from previous meetings are [here][15]
  * Previous meetings can be seen on the [Evolution Playlist on CHAOSStube][20]

Hope you can join us &#8211; it&#8217;s a great working group to be a part of!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
