### <span style="color: #993300;">Metrics Review Period is Open</span>

Now is the time for you to offer last-minute feedback on our metrics candidates prior to their release in April. You don&#8217;t have to have worked on metrics, or participated in the CHAOSS Community before; we value feedback from everyone. This release will include 8 new metrics and 1 metric that will be moving to a metrics model.

  * Occasional Contributors &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-occasional-contributors/> ([Issue for feedback][1])
  * Event Accessibility &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-event-accessibility/> ([Issue for feedback][2])
  * Project Demographics &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-project-demographics/> ([Issue for feedback][3])
  * Change Request Commits &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-change-request-commits/> ([Issue for feedback][4])
  * Change Request Reviews &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-change-request-reviews/> ([Issue for feedback][5])
  * Conversion Rate &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-conversion-rate>/ ([Issue for feedback][6])
  * Defect Resolution Time &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-defect-resolution-time/> ([Issue for feedback][7])
  * Contributor Recommendability &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-contributor-recommendibility/> ([Issue for feedback][8])
  * Project Popularity &#8211; <{{ baseURL }}metric-project-popularity> (moving to metric model)

Thanks for helping make our metrics the best they can be!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Interested in Mentoring? Let us Know!</span>

CHAOSS is a hopeful participant in a variety of student mentorship initiatives this year, including:

  * Google Summer of Code
  * Google Season of Docs
  * Outreachy
  * She Code Africa

We have an array of interesting proposed projects, and although we&#8217;re waiting on our official acceptance into these programs, we are hopeful that they will come to fruition. ### <span style="color: #993300;">Working Group Spotlight: Risk </span>

This week in the [Weekly Community meeting][10] we heard an update on our [Risk Working Group][11].

This group has been working on a metric called the [Defect Resolution Time][12] metric for the upcoming 2022-04 release. This metric aims to answer the question &#8220;How much time does a project take to resolve defects once they have been reported and recorded?&#8221; and you can add your feedback to [this issue][7] if you have any comments on this metric.

Next up for this group is working on cleaning up some repo labels and deciding what metric to work on next from the list of ideas in our [Metrics Spreadsheet][13]. Risk&#8217;s focus areas are Security, Business Risk, Code Quality, Licensing, Transparency, and Dependency Risk Assessment. If any of these areas sound interesting to you, here are the details on connecting with the Risk Working Group:

  * This group meets every other Thursday at 1:00 US Central/Chicago time at <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>. The next meeting is March 10.
  * Previous meetings can be watched on [CHAOSStube][14].
  * You can also join the [Risk WG Slack channel][15] and connect asynchronously.

The maintainer of this Working Group is Sean Goggins, so you should introduce yourself and say hello if you have any specific questions about the Risk Working Group.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
