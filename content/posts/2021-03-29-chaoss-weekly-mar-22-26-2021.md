### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSScast Episode 31: Marketing Metrics for OSS Foundations and Projects with Aniqa, Caroline, Kristi, and Paul from GNOME and KDE</span>

This week is packed with awesomeness, as we chat with some of the members of our App Ecosystem Working Group about metrics they use in their communities. Huge thanks to Aniqa Khokar, Caroline Henriksen, Kristi Progri, and Paul Brown for participating and offering their insight and experience.  Listen here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/31>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">GSOC Students: Apply Now!</span>

Students interested in applying for one of our project ideas can [now do so][1]! You can see the [list of ideas][2] and join the conversations. And you can read about the selection process [here][3]. Good luck to everyone and we can&#8217;t wait to meet you! :)\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
