### <span style="color: #993300;">Schedule is Published for CHAOSScon NA 2023!</span>

The CHAOSScon planning committee has released the schedule for CHAOSScon NA 2023, to be held in the **afternoon of May 9, 2023** in conjunction with Open Source Summit North America in Vancouver BC, Canada. You can view the schedule [here][1].

In-person registration is $20 USD and registration for OSSNA is a pre-requisite. Live streaming will be available for free, but registration for OSSNA virtual is required. You can register for both events on the [OSSNA registration page][2]. [Sponsorship][3] is also available, so if you&#8217;re interested in sponsoring, feel free to reach out.

Hope you can join us, either in-person or virtually!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Save the Date! CHAOSScon Africa June 14</span>

The CHAOSS Africa community is planning the very first CHAOSScon Africa for June 14! This will be in <span style="font-weight: 400;">Lagos, Nigeria and will be co-located with the <a href="https://festival.oscafrica.org/">Open Source Community Africa Festival.</a> Specific details for the event are still being coordinated, so stay tuned for more information! </span>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Next Badger Orientation April 12</span>

For anyone who is interested in participating in the next round of Badger Orientations, there is a meeting starting at **9:00 am US Central / Chicago time on Wednesday, April 12.** (This orientation will take up the second half of the regular DEI Badger meeting). In this meeting we will go over the badging process and how to review applications. If you are interested in becoming a new badger and would like to learn more, you can [fill out this form][4] and someone will contact you.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Evolution Working Group Rolled into Common</span>

In case you hadn&#8217;t been part of this discussion in the Weekly Community Call, metric development and discussions that had previously taken place in the Evolution Working Group will now happen in the Common Working Group, and this Working Group will cease to exist. Any metrics previously developed will remain in the [wg-evolution][5] repository, but this group will no longer hold meetings or develop any new metrics.

If you are interested in participating in &#8220;evolution&#8221; discussions, you are welcome to join the Common Working Group, which currently meets every other Thursday at **10:00 am** US Central/Chicago time. The next meeting is on **April 13**.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
