### <span style="color: #993300;">New Metric Model: Starter Project Health</span>

The Metrics Models Working Group released a new metric model: Starter Project Health. This Metrics Model is aimed to be a good place for an open source project to start, when taking a high level look at their project. There are four metrics in the model:

  * [Time to First Response][4] Determine the amount of time between when an activity was opened (e.g. Issue or Change Request) and when it received the first response from a human.

  * [Time to Close][5] Determine the amount of time between when an activity (e.g. Issue or Change Request) was created and when it was closed.
  * [Bus Factor][6] Determine the smallest number of people that make 50% of contributions
  * [Release Frequency][6] Determine the frequency of project releases (including point releases with bug fixes)

You can read more about the context around this model, and implementation on its [metric page][7].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

We have a confirmed date for CHAOSScon NA 2023! Hooray!

Registration is $20 USD and registration for OSSNA is a pre-requisite. You can register for both events on the [OSSNA registration page][8]. [Sponsorship][9] is also available, so if you&#8217;re interested in sponsoring, feel free to reach out.

The schedule is coming soon, so stay tuned for details. Hope to see you there!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Next Onboarding Session for New Chaotics</span>

The CHAOSS community continues to grow! To make the onboarding process a little easier, Elizabeth Barron (CHAOSS Community Manager) and Ruth Ikegah (CHAOSS Africa Community Lead) will be hosting the next Onboarding Session on March 1, 2023 at 11:00 am US Central/Chicago time**.** We know this time is not convenient for everyone, but this is just a start and we will add and iterate as we go.

This meeting will cover:

  * General overview of CHAOSS
  * How you can begin contributing
  * How to get started with using the metrics
  * Q&A

We hope you can join to meet other newcomers and learn about CHAOSS! You can [convert to your time zone here][10].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
