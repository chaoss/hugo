### <span style="color: #993300;">Announcing CHAOSS Africa!</span>

We have had consistent participation from the African community for a while, and we are very excited to now formalize our presence there! Ruth Ikegah will officially be leading the efforts to build the CHAOSS Africa community and working towards empowering people to contribute in multiple ways. If you are interested in participating or learning more, there is a new CHAOSS Africa Weekly Community Call beginning today (June 13, 2:00 pm WAT) and happening every Monday. More information to come, but you can also follow along with Ruth&#8217;s journey into community building by following CHAOSS Africa on Twitter ([@chaoss_africa][1]).

Congratulations Ruth! 🎉\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Mid-Year Break from CHAOSS Meetings</span>

CHAOSS will be taking a little break from regularly scheduled meetings **June 20-July 1 2022,** and all regular working group and community meetings will be canceled during this time. Regular working group and community meetings will resume **July 5, 2022**. (Any meetings between mentors and mentees for GSoC, GSoD, or Outreachy can still happen when needed. This cancellation only applies to our regular CHAOSS-wide meetings.) Enjoy your break!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Join Our Maintainer Burnout Session on June 17</span>

As part of GitHub&#8217;s Maintainer Month (June), CHAOSS is hosting a one-hour interactive workshop on Maintainer Burnout. This will be on **June 17, 2022 at 12pm US Central/Chicago time.**

<p style="padding-left: 40px;">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">Burnout is a relatable issue, almost everyone has been there– and it’s something we see quite often in open source. CHAOSS is hosting an interactive working group session to understand how maintainers manage burnout and talk about how to facilitate conversations about burnout and mental health in their open source communities. We will also discuss the</span><a href="{{ baseURL }}metric-project-burnout/"><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Project Burnout metric</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> from our DEI working group.</span>
</p>

You can [register for this free event][2], or if you would like to help with the event, please reach out to Ruth Ikegah on slack.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: You&#8217;re Invited! DEI Event Badging Reviewer Appreciation Event June 16</span>

This will be a fun, informal virtual event, and of course, there is no cost to participate. You can [register for the event here][4].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
