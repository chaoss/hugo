### <span style="color: #993300;">2022-04 Metrics Release is Official</span>

<div class="">
  The 2022-04 Metrics Release has now been officially shipped. 🎉 Huge thanks to all those from the working groups who worked on these metrics, and special extra thanks to Matt Germonprez and Yehui Wang for help with quality control, Vinod Ahuja for helping the working groups finalize changes, and Kevin Lumbard who is our release manager and keeps this whole machine rolling along. There are hours and hours of work behind the scenes for every metrics release and we are super grateful to the whole team for making sure things go according to plan.

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSScon EU 2022 CfP is Open! </span>

[CHAOSScon EU 2022][1] will be happening on September 12, in Dublin Ireland, in conjunction with [Open Source Summit Europe][2], and we are very pleased to announce that our CfP is open! We are interested in a variety of topics and presentation styles, so check out the [CfP details][1] on the CHAOSS website. **The deadline to apply is May 30**, so you have some time to draft that talk. We want to hear from you! (Sponsorship opportunities are also available, if you&#8217;d like more information about those too).\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">You&#8217;re Invited! DEI Event Badging Reviewer Appreciation Event June 16</span>

Please join us on **June 16, 2022 at 8:00 am US Central/Chicago time** ([convert to your time][3]) as we celebrate our amazing team of reviewers and the success of the DEI Badging Initiative! This will be a fun, informal virtual event, and of course, there is no cost to participate. You can [register for the event here][4].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Over 50 Badges Have Been Assigned</span>

The [DEI Event Badging Initiative][5] has assigned over 50 badges to open source events! The Initiative was officially launched in September 2020 and is so proud to have partnered with these event organizers to help center DEI in their events. Huge thanks go out to our whole team of reviewers, who volunteer their time to review and provide feedback on all applications. Special thanks to Matt Cantu Snell, who has grown and shepherded this program from its infancy and continues to be an integral part of the automated processes and badging bot. Amazing work everyone! Keep on badgin&#8217;!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Mentorship Program Deadlines for Outreachy and GSoC Approaching</span>

If you are one of our student mentorship program applicants for Outreachy and Google Summer of Code, this is a reminder for you that the deadlines to register your interest and apply are fast approaching!

  * Google Summer of Code: Applications are due **April 19** (Make sure you read the [GSoC Guidelines][6] and the [CHAOSS Requirements][7])
  * Outreachy: Applications are due **April 22** (Make sure you read the [Outreachy Final Applications Guidelines][8] and the [CHAOSS Requirements][9])

Best of luck to all our mentee applicants. We are glad you&#8217;re in the CHAOSS community!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
