### <span style="color: #993300;">Volunteer Opportunity: Become a Badger and Help Badge Events for Their DEI Efforts</span>

The DEI Event Badging Initiative is looking for more Badgers! If you would like to help review applications and assign badges, please join us for more information on **May 18 at 12:00 pm US Central/Chicago time** on our Zoom channel: <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Volunteer Opportunity: Help With Our Maintainer Burnout Session </span>

As part of GitHub&#8217;s Maintainer Month (June), CHAOSS is hosting a one-hour interactive workshop on Maintainer Burnout. This is in the early stages of planning, but you can find more information in the [initial planning document][1]. We are currently working toward confirming a date and time, and the agenda. If you would like to help with this event, please reach out to Ruth Ikegah on CHAOSS slack.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Mentorships Being Finalized </span>

We are currently finalizing the selected applicants for three mentorship programs: Google Summer of Code, Outreachy, and Google Season of Docs. Once the applicants have been chosen and all applicants have been notified, we will be announcing our selections. We are extremely grateful to all who applied, and we hope you will stay with the CHAOSS community in some capacity. We would highly value any contributions you would like to make!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: You&#8217;re Invited! DEI Event Badging Reviewer Appreciation Event June 16</span>

Please join us on **June 16, 2022 at 8:00 am US Central/Chicago time** ([convert to your time][2]) as we celebrate our amazing team of reviewers and the success of the DEI Badging Initiative! This will be a fun, informal virtual event, and of course, there is no cost to participate. You can [register for the event here][3].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: CHAOSScon CfP closes May 30</span>

If you are interested in submitting a session for CHAOSScon on September 12 in Dublin, Ireland, please send that to us on or before **May 30, 2022**. We are looking for a variety of topics, including:

  * metrics strategy
  * deep dive into the use of metrics
  * failure of metrics in practice
  * lessons learned, use cases, and case studies
  * proposals for new metrics
  * new visualizations

We are also looking for a few different formats and presentation styles such as tutorials, walkthroughs, lightning talks, and regular sessions. We are accepting submissions for virtual and in-person talks, and you can [submit your abstract through Google Forms][4].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
