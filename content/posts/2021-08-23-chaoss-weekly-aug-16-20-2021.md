### <span style="color: #993300;">Metrics Models Working Group Will Continue </span>

After a very successful first meeting last week, it was decided to continue the Metrics Models Working Group meetings on a weekly basis (at least to start with). You can read the notes from the first meeting [here][1].

This group will continue to meet **Tuesdays at 6:00 pm US Central/Chicago time (Wednesdays at 7:00 am, Beijing Standard Time)**. (We realize this time is inconvenient for our European Community, so we encourage you to bring your thoughts to the next App Ecosystem Group meeting on **August 23 at 12:00 pm US Central/Chicago time**.)

Both calls happen at our Community Zoom channel: <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>. Please join us!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Next Metrics Release Dates</span>

The upcoming metrics freeze is upon us! The October release schedule is as follows:

  * **September 1, 2021**: Metrics development freeze.
  * **September 1 &#8211; September 30**: Public feedback period on proposed metrics.
  * **October 1, 2021** (Approx): 2021-10 Metrics release.

All working groups should strive to bring closure to metrics currently in progress by September 1 if possible.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
