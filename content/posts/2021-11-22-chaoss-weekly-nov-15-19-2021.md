### <span style="color: #993300;">End-of-Year Meeting Schedule</span>

We wanted to make sure everyone is aware of the end-of-year meeting schedule for various CHAOSS community groups.

CHAOSS Office Hours, Working Groups & Community Meeting:

  * No meetings November 22-26, 2021 (US Thanksgiving holiday)
  * No meetings December 10, 2021-January 10, 2022 (End-of-year Break)

CHAOSS App Ecosystem Working Group:

  * No meetings between now and January 10, 2022

DEI Badging and DEI Badging Outreach:

  * No meetings between now and January 4, 2022

The calendar on our [Participate Page][1] should be up-to-date, but if you have any questions at all, please feel free to comment in [Slack][2] or on the [mailing list][3]. There are a few of us that are usually around, regardless. 🙂 Happy holidays!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSScast China Launched! </span>

Huge thank you to Xiaoya Xia who is spearheading the launch of CHAOSScast&#8217;s sister podcast in Chinese! This podcast is available here<span style="font-weight: 400;"> </span>[<span style="font-weight: 400;">https://www.ximalaya.com/keji/54433824/</span>][4] so if you have ideas for guests or topics to be discussed please reach out! There are several ways for you to connect with our Chinese community, all listed on our [Participate Page][1]. Congratulations to Xiaoya for bringing CHAOSScast to a new level! We are extremely excited about this new venture.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Learning More About Contributing to CHAOSS</span>

Several weeks ago, Matt Cantu published a [blog post][5] about common ways people connect with and join the CHAOSS community. The idea is to make this a more visible and permanent part of our website, but in the meantime, it&#8217;s a great guide to just getting started with us. You can read the blog post [here][5] and if you&#8217;re new to the community, it&#8217;s highly recommended reading. Thanks, Matt!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Joining the CHAOSS Community</span>

We are trying something new in the CHAOSS Community! If you&#8217;d like to get more involved with the community but aren&#8217;t sure where to start, we have a handy [form for you to fill out][6]. After that, our Community Manager (Elizabeth) will reach out to you with some guidance on where to go next. We&#8217;d love to have you participate!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Come Join us in Slack!</span>

CHAOSS has revived our Slack channel as a form of connecting with our community and holding space for newcomers to ask questions. If you like Slack, join us here! <https://join.slack.com/t/chaoss-workspace/shared_invite/zt-r65szij9-QajX59hkZUct82b0uACA6g>. Join our #office-hours channel if you have questions, or just pop in our #general channel to say hello! (Also, please note, we have updated the link, so now it won&#8217;t expire!)\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
