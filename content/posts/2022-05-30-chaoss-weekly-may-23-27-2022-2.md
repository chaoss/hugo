### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: CHAOSScon CfP closes TODAY!</span>

If you are interested in submitting a session for CHAOSScon on September 12 in Dublin, Ireland, please send that to us on or before **May 30, 2022**. We are looking for a variety of topics, including:

  * metrics strategy
  * deep dive into the use of metrics
  * failure of metrics in practice
  * lessons learned, use cases, and case studies
  * proposals for new metrics
  * new visualizations

We are also looking for a few different formats and presentation styles such as tutorials, walkthroughs, lightning talks, and regular sessions. We are accepting submissions for virtual and in-person talks, and you can [submit your abstract through Google Forms][1].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Tell Us About Your Experience with the CHAOSS Website</span>

In preparation for our website redesign happening this summer, we are doing a lightweight, high-level study of our community member&#8217;s experience with the CHAOSS site. We would love your input through this [quick 3 minute survey][2]. Thank you! 🙏\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Join Our Maintainer Burnout Session on June 17</span>

As part of GitHub&#8217;s Maintainer Month (June), CHAOSS is hosting a one-hour interactive workshop on Maintainer Burnout. This will be on **June 17, 2022 at 12pm US Central/Chicago time.**

<p style="padding-left: 40px;">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">Burnout is a relatable issue, almost everyone has been there– and it’s something we see quite often in open source. CHAOSS is hosting an interactive working group session to understand how maintainers manage burnout and talk about how to facilitate conversations about burnout and mental health in their open source communities. We will also discuss the</span><a href="{{ baseURL }}metric-project-burnout/"><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Project Burnout metric</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> from our DEI working group.</span>
</p>

You can [register for this free event][3], or if you would like to help with the event, please reach out to Ruth Ikegah on slack.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: You&#8217;re Invited! DEI Event Badging Reviewer Appreciation Event June 16</span>

Please join us on **June 16, 2022 at 8:00 am US Central/Chicago time** ([convert to your time][4]) as we celebrate our amazing team of reviewers and the success of the DEI Badging Initiative! This will be a fun, informal virtual event, and of course, there is no cost to participate. You can [register for the event here][5].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
