### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: End-of-Year Meeting Schedule</span>

We wanted to make sure everyone is aware of the end-of-year meeting schedule for various CHAOSS community groups.

CHAOSS Office Hours, Working Groups & Community Meeting:

  * No meetings December 10, 2021-January 10, 2022

CHAOSS App Ecosystem Working Group:

  * No meetings between now and January 10, 2022

DEI Badging and DEI Badging Outreach:

  * No meetings between now and January 4, 2022

The calendar on our [Participate Page][1] should be up-to-date, but if you have any questions at all, please feel free to comment in [Slack][2] or on the [mailing list][3]. There are a few of us that are usually around, regardless. 🙂 Happy holidays!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

This week&#8217;s podcast took a little different approach and celebrated the CHAOSS community with Georg Link, Sean Goggins, Matt Germonprez, Willem Jiang, Nicole Huesman, Xiaoya Xia, Dawn Foster, and Elizabeth Barron. Join us as we talk about all things CHAOSS in 2021:

  * Mentorship programs
  * Software updates
  * International growth
  * CHAOSScast
  * Metrics & the release process
  * DEI Badging and reflection
  * Data ethics
  * CHAOSScon

And more! This was personally very fun to be a part of. If you&#8217;d like a quick recap of everything that&#8217;s been going on with CHAOSS, this is the episode for you! <https://podcast.chaoss.community/49>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
