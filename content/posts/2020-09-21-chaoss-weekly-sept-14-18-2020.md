### <span style="color: #980000">2 New Initiatives Released! </span>

<p dir="ltr">
  This was a very busy week for the CHAOSS team! This week we released 2 brand new initiatives: our D&I Badging Program and our Community Health Reports. Congratulations to all who were involved in making these happen! If you would like to help us spread the word, you can tweet these, or share them on LinkedIn or any other social media places you happen to be:
</p>

  * [Community Health Reports Announcement][1]
  * [D&I Badging Program Announcement][2]

Thanks for helping us spread our good news! 🎉🙌

<p dir="ltr">
  <!--more-->
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">CHAOSScast Episode 18: DevRel Metrics with Mary Thengvall</span>

We were very excited to have Mary Thengvall from Camunda as our guest on CHAOSScast. Mary joins Matt Broberg, Armstrong Foundjem, and Nicole Huesman to share her experience with all things developer relations and building metrics to help measure success without feeding into unintended consequences. If you&#8217;ve ever wondered about the DevRel world, listen now: [https://podcast.chaoss.community/18.][3]\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">CHAOSScast Sponsors Needed</span>

As CHAOSScast will be switching to a bi-weekly schedule soon, we are on the lookout for anyone who might be interested in sponsoring our awesome podcast! Funding is needed for editing episodes and providing show notes, and for podcast hosting. If you or your company would like more information, please reach out to Georg Link (georg@chaoss.community) for a sponsorship prospectus. It&#8217;s for an awesome cause!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
