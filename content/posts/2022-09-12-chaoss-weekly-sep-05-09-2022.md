### <span style="color: #993300;">Announcing the CHAOSS Community Survey! </span>

During the opening remarks at CHAOSScon, the CHAOSS Community Survey was announced! We highly encourage everyone in the CHAOSS community to take this survey and share their thoughts and experiences with being a part of the CHAOSS community.

From our [blog post announcement][1]:

> A core value of CHAOSS is centering diversity, equity, and inclusion in everything we do. In order to make our community more welcoming and inclusive and continue to center DEI, we have been working with our DEI Audit team to develop a <a href="https://chaossproject.limesurvey.net/835156?lang=en" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">community survey</a>. With this survey, we hope to increase our understanding of community members’ experiences within CHAOSS, and surface areas for improving our policies and practices.

Please feel free to read the [full blog post][1] for more details on the survey, and help us make our community more welcoming and inclusive for all!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">That&#8217;s a Wrap! CHAOSSconEU 2022 was Awesome!</span>

Today was CHAOSSconEU 2022, a co-located event with Open Source Summit in Dublin, Ireland. With around 45 people in attendance either in-person or via the livestream, we heard talks on a variety of topics from being a newcomer in the CHAOSS project to the technical limitations of datasets used for measuring community health.

We will be releasing the videos and slides, but in the meantime please enjoy this photo of the whole Dublin CHAOSSconEU crew. We are grateful for all our attendees, speakers, sponsors, and all those who made the event possible!
