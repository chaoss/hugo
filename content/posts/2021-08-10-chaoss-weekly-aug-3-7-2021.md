### <span style="color: #993300;">Only a Few Days Left to Submit to CHAOSScon!</span>

The CHAOSScon CfP will be open until **August 13**, so you have just a few more days to [submit a talk][1]! Some suggested topics are:

<a id="user-content-software-related-metrics-topics" class="anchor" href="https://chaoss.local/chaosscon-2021-na/#software-related-metrics-topics" aria-hidden="true"></a>**Software related metrics topics:**

  * tutorials
  * walk throughs
  * demos
  * how-to&#8217;s
  * new features

#### <a id="user-content-metrics-related-topics" class="anchor" href="https://chaoss.local/chaosscon-2021-na/#metrics-related-topics" aria-hidden="true"></a>Metrics related topics:

  * metrics strategy
  * deep dive into the use of metrics
  * failure of metrics in practice
  * lessons learned, use cases, and case studies
  * proposals for new metrics
  * new visualizations

Do you have something to share in one of these areas? [We highly encourage you to send in an abstract.][1]

You can also now register for [Open Source Summit][2], and the CHAOSScon add-on. CHAOSScon is happening in-person (and virtually) September 30 in Seattle, immediately following the OSS conference.

Sponsorship opportunities are also available! We&#8217;d be incredibly grateful for your company&#8217;s support, and your sponsorship will help us improve our DEI efforts for the conference.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Upcoming CHAOSS Community Talks at OSSummit and OSPOCon</span>

Some members of the CHAOSS community have upcoming talks at conferences, so we&#8217;d like to help them spread the word! If you&#8217;re attending the upcoming OSSummit/OSPOCon, we encourage you to check them out:

  * [_A Beginners Guide to Open Source Metrics and_ Analysis][7] (Sophia Vargas)
  * [_Panel Discussion: Contributor Leaderboards to Incentivize Good Community Citizenship_][8] (Georg Link, Amy Marrich, Matthew Tift, Matt Germonprez, Sean Goggins)
  * _[Panel Discussion: Eliminating Common Barriers to Contribution for More Inclusive Communities][9] _(Georg Link, Daniel Izquierdo, Anita Sarma, Mariam Guizani)

If you have an upcoming talk and would like to include it in the CHAOSSweekly, let Elizabeth know or [add it to our spreadsheet][10].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #800000;">Next Metrics Release Dates</span>

As a point of note, the upcoming metric release schedule is as follows:

  * **September 1, 2021**: Metrics development freeze.
  * **September 1 &#8211; September 30**: Public feedback period on proposed metrics.
  * **October 1, 2021** (Approx): 2021-10 Metrics release.

All working groups should strive to bring closure to metrics currently in progress by September 1 if possible.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
