### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSS Meetup Shanghai December 27</span>

Our Asia-Pacific community is hosting a meetup/workshop on December 27 in Shanghai. They are still finalizing the details, but it will feature some pre-recorded talks and various breakout sessions for those interested in learning about CHAOSS and talking about metrics. We&#8217;ll pass along details as they become available, but if you&#8217;d like to join the planning discussion or learn more about it, the Asia-Pacific Community meets at **8:00 am Central Standard Time on Wednesday, December 16**, and you&#8217;re welcome to join!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Evolution, Risk, Augur Meeting Changes for 2021</span>

<div id="day-20201201" class="day ">
  <div class="date-label">
    The Evolution, Risk, and Augur working groups have decided to shift their meeting times, starting in 2021.
  </div>
  
  <ul>
    <li>
      Risk WG will be 10-10:50 am Central (Chicago) Time every other Tuesday
    </li>
    <li>
      Evolution WG will be 11:00-11:50 am Central (Chicago) Time every other Wednesday
    </li>
    <li>
      Augur will be 11:00-11:50 am Central (Chicago) Time every other Thursday
    </li>
  </ul>
  
  <p>
    We will continue to list the upcoming meetings in this newsletter and through the website here: <a href="{{ baseURL }}participate/">{{ baseURL }}participate/</a>.
  </p>
</div>

### <span style="color: #980000;">Documentation Accessibility and Project Burnout Metrics Released for Review</span>

<div id="day-20201201" class="day ">
  <div class="date-label">
    In case you missed it, the D&I Working Group released 2 new metrics for community review during our continuous release cycle. They are <a href="{{ baseURL }}metric-documentation-accessibility/">Documentation Accessibility</a> and <a href="{{ baseURL }}metric-project-burnout/">Project Burnout</a>. A lot of hard work went into developing those metrics and the community would love your 👀 and 💭 on them!
  </div>
</div>
