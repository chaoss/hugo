### <span style="color: #993300;">Minor Changes to Monthly Meeting</span>

We decided at the last CHAOSS Community Weekly Meeting to change the format of our Monthly Meetings. Previously, these meetings were held on the first Tuesday of the month during our weekly community call, and they were meant to provide a brief summary of what&#8217;s happening in each of the working groups. We decided to roll these updates into the natural flow of the weekly community meetings instead of lumping them all into one giant meeting. Moving forward, you can expect to see working group updates peppered in amongst the regular weekly meeting agendas.

And just as a reminder, weekly meetings happen on Tuesdays at 11:00 am US Central/Chicago Time Zone. You can join them via our [Zoom Channel][1] and you can [check your timezone here][2]. These meetings are a great way to get to know the CHAOSS community and what we&#8217;re all about. If you&#8217;re new to the community we highly recommend attending, if you&#8217;re able to!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Joining the CHAOSS Community</span>

We are trying something new in the CHAOSS Community! If you&#8217;d like to get more involved with the community but aren&#8217;t sure where to start, we have a handy [form for you to fill out][3]. After that, our Community Manager (Elizabeth) will reach out to you with some guidance on where to go next. We&#8217;d love to have you participate!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Come Join us in Slack!</span>

CHAOSS has revived our Slack channel as a form of connecting with our community and holding space for newcomers to ask questions. If you like Slack, join us here! <https://join.slack.com/t/chaoss-workspace/shared_invite/zt-r65szij9-QajX59hkZUct82b0uACA6g>. Join our #office-hours channel if you have questions, or just pop in our #general channel to say hello! (Also, please note, we have updated the link, so now it won&#8217;t expire!)\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
