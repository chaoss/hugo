### <span style="color: #800000;">No Meetings Today</span>

The App Ecosystem Meeting has been canceled today for the US Holiday. CHAOSS meetings will resume in full force tomorrow. See you then!

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #800000;">Open Zoom Office Hours Starting on June 7</span>

Beginning June 7, we will be hosting open &#8220;office hours&#8221; sessions for newcomers who are joining the project. For those who aren&#8217;t familiar with this term, it&#8217;s a time when one of the CHAOSS community will be openly available on Zoom to answer any questions you have. No appointment needed; you can just hop on the CHAOSS Community Zoom call any time during the 2-hour window, and leave when your question has been answered.

  * When: Every Monday morning, beginning June 7
  * Time: 9:00 am &#8211; 11:00 am US Central (Chicago) Time
  * Where: <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>

Come say hello and we&#8217;ll help you find what you need!

And if you&#8217;re already a member of the CHAOSS community and would like to help out with this initiative, you can sign up for a 2-hour slot [here][1].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Changes to Upcoming Workshops for Getting Started as a Contributor to CHAOSS Software</span>

Take note! We are making a few changes to our upcoming workshop series, so we encourage you to have a look. All workshops will be hosted on our Community Zoom (<https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>) and are from 8am &#8211; 10am CDT, 6:30pm &#8211; 8:30pm IST. You can join as an attendee, or a mentor! GSoC Students are also highly encouraged to attend.

  * June 5, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Restful API Development][2]
  * **Added**: June 8, 2021- [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS & Intro to Github Workflow)][3]
  * **Canceled, being rescheduled (date TBD):** June 19, 2021 &#8211;  [Jupyter Notebooks: Getting Started][4]
  * July 17, 2021 &#8211; [Jupyter Notebook Visualizations][5]
  * July 31, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS & Intro to Github Workflow)][6]

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
