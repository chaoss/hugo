### <span style="color: #980000;">Welcome Back, Friends! </span>

Hope you all had a relaxing and safe holiday/end of year break! We resumed meetings last week and are easing back into things. We have a lot of exciting things ahead for the CHAOSS project, and we&#8217;d love your input, your ideas, and your participation. Hope to see you soon at a meeting, on the GitHub repos, or on the mailing list!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Metrics Cycle Moved to March</span>

Here&#8217;s a reminder that it was decided in our weekly community call a few months ago that it would be easier for a lot of people if we shifted the metrics release to March. Given that there&#8217;s a 30 day public review period on all metrics prior to the release, all working groups should keep in mind that any metrics in progress should be finished by end of January if possible.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
