### <span style="color: #993300;">Successful Grace Hopper Open Source Day!</span>

We had the opportunity to take part in Grace Hopper Conference&#8217;s first virtual [Open Source Day][1] through the Augur project, and it was a rousing success! We were able to include more first-time contributors than we ever have in the past. A big thank you goes out to [Srishti Guleria][2] for assisting as a mentor, and Sean Goggins for guiding the way. We hope to continue our participation in future GHC Open Source Day events!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSScast Episode #39: Leaderboards and Metrics at Drupal.org with Matthew Tift and Tim Lehnen</span>

This week we were delighted to host Matthew Tift and Tim Lehnen from the Drupal project on CHAOSScast. Host Georg Link and panelist Nicole Huesman joined Matthew and Tim to discuss all the amazing work Drupal has done to improve community health, inclusivity and engagement, and the potential pitfalls of using leaderboards as measures to track contributions. They also talked about how to measure non-code contributions and tracking and measuring corporate sponsorship and influence. This was a great episode that you can listen to here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/39>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #800000;">Reminder: Zoom Office Hours Now Open Every Monday </span>

Last week, we started hosting open office hours on Mondays for newcomers to the CHAOSS project. For those who aren&#8217;t familiar with this term, it&#8217;s a time when one of the CHAOSS community will be openly available on Zoom to answer any questions you have. No registration needed; you can just hop on the CHAOSS Community Zoom call any time during the 2-hour window, and leave when your question has been answered.

  * When: Every Monday, beginning June 7
  * Time: 9:00 am &#8211; 11:00 am US Central (Chicago) Time
  * Where: <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>

Come say hello and we&#8217;ll help you find what you need!

And here&#8217;s a reminder that if you&#8217;re already a member of the CHAOSS community and would like to help out with this initiative, you can sign up for a 2-hour slot [here][6].
