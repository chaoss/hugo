### <span style="color: #800000;">CHAOSScast Episode 36: DEI Badging with Rachel Braun and Celia Stamps </span>

We were absolutely thrilled to host the amazing Rachel Braun and Celia Stamps from the Linux Foundation Events team in this latest CHAOSScast episode! Georg Link, Ruth Ikegah, Matt Germonprez, and Matt Cantu joined them to chat about the CHAOSS DEI Badging Initiative and their experience with going through the process multiple times. They discussed why it&#8217;s important to bring DEI metrics like these into practice, and how partnering with event organizers on an initiative like this helps everybody. We are super grateful for Rachel and Celia&#8217;s time, and you can listen to this episode here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/36>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #800000;">Zoom Office Hours Now Open Every Monday </span>

Yesterday, we hosted our first open &#8220;office hours&#8221; session for newcomers who are joining the project. For those who aren&#8217;t familiar with this term, it&#8217;s a time when one of the CHAOSS community will be openly available on Zoom to answer any questions you have. No appointment needed; you can just hop on the CHAOSS Community Zoom call any time during the 2-hour window, and leave when your question has been answered.

  * When: Every Monday, beginning June 7
  * Time: 9:00 am &#8211; 11:00 am US Central (Chicago) Time
  * Where: <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>

Come say hello and we&#8217;ll help you find what you need!

And here&#8217;s a reminder that if you&#8217;re already a member of the CHAOSS community and would like to help out with this initiative, you can sign up for a 2-hour slot [here][1].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Continuous Metrics Release: New Metrics Under Review</span>

All the Working Groups have been hard at work, continuing to develop new metrics for use in measuring community health. In between our regular releases (which happen in March and September/October), we have what is called a &#8220;Continuous Metrics Release&#8221; so metrics developed during this time don&#8217;t have to wait months to be seen.

Currently open for review are these metrics, with more on the way!

  * [Documentation Discoverability][2] (DEI Working Group)

Feel free to read through these metrics and make any comments or offer feedback. (These metrics will still be released under the community review period during the next release.)\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
