### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: CHAOSS Meetings Resume This Week</span>

We took a little break last week from our usually scheduled meetings, but all regular meetings resume this week. A full list can be seen below and all meetings are held on our CHAOSS Zoom channel: <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>. Hope you can join us!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Take the CHAOSS Community Survey</span>

Last week we announced that the CHAOSS Community Survey wsa open! We highly encourage everyone in the CHAOSS community to take this survey and share their thoughts and experiences with being a part of the CHAOSS community.

From our [blog post announcement][1]:

> A core value of CHAOSS is centering diversity, equity, and inclusion in everything we do. In order to make our community more welcoming and inclusive and continue to center DEI, we have been working with our DEI Audit team to develop a <a href="https://chaossproject.limesurvey.net/835156?lang=en" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">community survey</a>. With this survey, we hope to increase our understanding of community members’ experiences within CHAOSS, and surface areas for improving our policies and practices.

Please feel free to read the [full blog post][1] for more details on the survey, and help us make our community more welcoming and inclusive for all!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Volunteer Opportunity: CHAOSScon Video Editing</span>

We are looking for someone to help us make the in-person talks at CHAOSScon available for everyone! If you have experience with video editing software and would be able to separate the individual talks and add some CHAOSS brand elements, we would appreciate the help. Once we have talks broken into individual videos, we can upload them to YouTube. We estimate the video editing will take 1-2 hours, and will make a huge impact in helping others learn from the talks that were given at CHAOSScon. Please reach out to Georg Link or Elizabeth Barron on Slack if you are able to take this on.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">New Onboarding Meeting for Newcomers to CHAOSS</span>

The CHAOSS community continues to grow! To make the onboarding process a little easier, Elizabeth Barron (CHAOSS Community Manager) and Ruth Ikegah (CHAOSS Africa Community Lead) will be hosting onboarding meetings the **first Wednesday of every month at 11:00 am US Central/Chicago time, starting October 5.** We know this time is not convenient for everyone, but this is just a start and we will add and iterate as we go.

This meeting will cover:

  * General overview of CHAOSS
  * How you can begin contributing
  * How to get started with using the metrics
  * Q&A

We hope you can join to meet other newcomers and learn about CHAOSS! You can [convert to your time zone here][4].\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
