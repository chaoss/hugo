### <span style="color: #980000;">CHAOSScast Episode 26: The SustainOSS Crossover Episode!</span>

This week&#8217;s CHAOSScast was an exciting crossover episode, jointly produced by CHAOSS and Sustain (our kind sponsors!). SustainOSS is all about holding space for conversations around the sustainability of the open source ecosystem, and community health is a huge part of that. Join Venia Logan, Georg Link, and Brian Profitt from CHAOSScast, and Pia Mancini and Richard Littauer from Sustain where they talk about qualitative metrics, community building, and running the Sustain events. You can find it here: <https://podcast.chaoss.community/26>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Evolution WG Meeting Weekly in January</span>

The Evolution Working Group will be meeting every week in January, to accelerate progress on outstanding issues and metrics. Please join us if this is something you&#8217;re interested in! This group meets on Wednesdays at 9:00 am Central.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Metrics Close for Review End of January</span>

Here&#8217;s a reminder that all working groups should be working to close any metrics in progress by end of January if possible. This will allow for our 30-day public review cycle prior to our release in March 2021. Thank you! You&#8217;re doing amazing work!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
