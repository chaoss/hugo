### <span style="color: #800000;">Welcome Google Summer of Code Students!</span>

### <span style="color: #800000;">CHAOSSCon Planning Has Begun, and We Need Your Input!</span>

The CHAOSSCon planning committee has started developing ideas for this year&#8217;s CHAOSSCon. We would love your input! Please give us your thoughts here: <https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdBfNcooWnmdq4x4k8E__cObvvzYLtLMY8T2EO3vB-VElb1ZQ/viewform>

We are in the early stages of planning but will be using time at the end of each week&#8217;s Community Call to move ideas forward. Keep an eye out for updates and we can&#8217;t wait to see you in person!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #800000;">Zoom Office Hours Starting Monday, June 7</span>

Sean Goggins, Matt Germonprez, and me (Elizabeth Barron) will be hosting a weekly open Zoom call from 9:00-11:00 US Central (Chicago) time on Monday mornings.  We will start the calls on June 7, so if you&#8217;re new to the project or just want to pop in to ask questions, we&#8217;ll be there! We&#8217;re happy to chat with you. You can join anytime here, and of course, you don&#8217;t need to stick around for the full 2-hour window: <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #800000;">Reminder: Come Join us in Slack!</span>

CHAOSS has revived our Slack channel as a form of connecting with our community and holding space for newcomers to ask questions. If you like Slack, join us here! <https://join.slack.com/t/chaoss-workspace/shared_invite/zt-ptka3wu9-ey~fd3lh7gj2659WVVzFMQ>. Join our #office-hours channel if you have questions, or just pop in our #general channel to say hello!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000;">Upcoming Workshops for Getting Started as a Contributor to CHAOSS Software</span>

In case you missed it, we have successfully launched a series of free virtual workshops to help you get started contributing to CHAOSS software (mostly centered on Augur). All workshops will be hosted on our Community Zoom (<https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>) and are from 8am &#8211; 10am CDT, 6:30pm &#8211; 8:30pm IST. You can join as an attendee, or a mentor! GSoC Students are also highly encouraged to attend.

  * June 5, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Restful API Development][1]
  * June 19, 2021 &#8211;  [Jupyter Notebooks: Getting Started][2]
  * July 17, 2021 &#8211; [Jupyter Notebook Visualizations][3]
  * July 31, 2021 &#8211; [Workshop: Configuring Your Computer (Getting Setup for Python Development in CHAOSS & Intro to Github Workflow)][4]

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
