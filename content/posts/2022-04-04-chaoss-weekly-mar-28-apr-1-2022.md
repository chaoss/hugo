### <span style="color: #993300;">Metrics Release Update</span>

Now that the 30-day public comment period is over and the Working Groups have made their final changes, there are a few minor tweaks to be made to all the metrics before the 2022-04 release. The release will happen in 1-2 weeks, so stay tuned! Thanks to all who provided final feedback and review. You all get many gold stars! ⭐️\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSS Migrating From Mailing Lists Toward Forums</span>

After much discussion, we have decided to move away from the traditional mailing list mode of communication and move toward a forum, to better facilitate open discussion and make it easier for newcomers to join conversations in progress. There is a committee of 10 who are working on this issue, with representation from a variety of the different parts of CHAOSS. We have an open ad-hoc Slack channel set up ([#forum-migration-planning][1]) so if you have experience migrating from mailing lists to Discourse, and you have feedback or advice we would love you to join us. We are just starting this journey! (And just for clarification, we will continue to use Slack for more immediate conversations and chat.)\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Working Group Spotlight: DEI Working Group</span>

This week in the Community Call, we heard updates from the [DEI Working Group][2]. This group looks at metrics that have relevance to diversity, equity, and inclusion within open source. A sub-group is focused on our DEI Event Badging Initiative.

This group included 2 new metrics candidates in the latest release:

<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="3">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">Project Demographics: </span><a href="https://github.com/chaoss/wg-dei/issues/402"><span style="font-weight: 400;">https://github.com/chaoss/wg-dei/issues/402</span></a>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="3">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">Event Accessibility: </span><a href="https://github.com/chaoss/wg-dei/issues/401"><span style="font-weight: 400;">https://github.com/chaoss/wg-dei/issues/401</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> (will also be added to the DEI Event Badging Initiative)</span>
</li>

This group also has been experimenting with the [GitHub Project Board][3] as a way to organize and categorize incoming issues and to-dos. As well, there is work going on in this group around creating a more comprehensive approach to the various mentorship programs we participate in.

DEI Working Group meets every Wednesday at 10:00 am US Central/Chicago time and the next meeting is **Wednesday, April 6**. Hope to see you there!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
