### <span style="color: #980000">CHAOSScast Episode 12: How Social Marketing Interfaces with Community with Michelle Dalton</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  This week we spent time with Michelle Dalton to speak about community management, communication, and how the world of digital marketing and building relationships can inform and affect open source community management. This was an excellent podcast and we are so happy to host Michelle this week! Please have a listen: <a href="https://podcast.chaoss.community/12">https://podcast.chaoss.community/12</a><!--more-->
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">CHAOSS And GNOME Talk at GUADEC Now Available</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  GNOME&#8217;s Sriram Ramkrishna and our very own Georg Link co-presented a talk at this year&#8217;s GUADEC (the GNOME conference) on building community metrics. Their talk was very well-received and is <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qYzqsDHp09s&list=PLkmRdYgttscE1mTtyTvj0hx34gVAKpC4H&index=9&t=0s">now available on YouTube</a> for the rest of us to enjoy. Great job, Georg and Sri! You are awesome!
</p>
