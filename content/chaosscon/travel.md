# Visiting Brussels

There are several excellent resources for visitors and tourists to Brussels. The [visit.brussels website][1] contains information on everything you need to know, including:

  * ✈️ [Traveling to and from the city][2]
  * 🏨 [Where to stay][3]
  * 🍽️ [Restaurants, bars, and cafes][4]
  * 💃 [Things to do][5]

The [city of Brussels][6] also provides helpful information for tourists including:

  * 🗺️ [City maps][7]
  * 👮 [Police and emergency services info][8]
  * 🌍 [Embassies and Consulates][9]
  * 💻 [How to connect to free city-wide Wifi][10]

You can also view a [listing of events][11] happening in Brussels during the week and weekend of FOSDEM.

# Other Nearby Open Source Events

CHAOSScon is loosely co-located with [FOSDEM][12] but we encourage you to check out these other events happening in and around the area.

  * [FOSDEM Fringe][13] - a comprehensive list of FOSDEM related events happening immediately before and after FOSDEM
  * [EU Open Source Week][14] - these events are happening in Brussels between Jan 26, 2026 and Feb 1, 2026
  * [EU Open Source Policy Summit 2026][15] - happening in Brussels Jan 30, 2026

CHAOSScon is also a part of the [Open Source Village][16] a two week celebration of open source hosted by our friends at the Commons Hub. There are many events happening during this time, and you can purchase an all-inclusive ticket along with your CHAOSScon ticket. You can read more about this on the [CHAOSScon EU page][17].
