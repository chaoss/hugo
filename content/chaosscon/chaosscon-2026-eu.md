## About CHAOSScon

Learn about open source project health metrics and tools used by open source projects, communities, and engineering teams to track and analyze their community work. This conference will provide a venue for discussing open source project health, CHAOSS updates, use cases, and hands-on workshops for developers, community managers, project managers, and anyone interested in measuring open source project health. We will also share insights from the CHAOSS context working groups including OSPOs, University Open Source, and Open Source in Science and Research.

### 🎟️ Registration

There are two ticket levels for this year's CHAOSScon:

#### CHAOSScon EU 2026: €40.00

This ticket gives you full access to CHAOSScon. You can attend all talks, panels, and interactive sessions throughout the day. 

This year's ticket also includes lunch, coffee and snacks, and the evening social event, as well as access to dedicated rooms for hands-on and small group discussions, which is something we've not provided in years past. We are excited about this new format!

#### CHAOSScon EU 2026 + Open Source Village: €121.00

This ticket includes everything in the CHAOSScon EU ticket, plus access to _all_ Open Source Village Events happening in the Commons Hub for the two week period surrounding FOSDEM.

This is a combined ticket that offers a 33% savings compared to buying the CHAOSScon and Open Source Village tickets separately, while giving you a broader open source experience beyond just the conference day. To learn more about these events, visit <https://opensourcevillage.org/>.

## Event Details

### Code of Conduct at Event

All speakers and attendees are required to adhere to CHAOSS's [Code of Conduct][4]. Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported in accordance with our [Procedure for Making a Code of Conduct Report][5]. You can also read our [Incident Response Plan][6] for more information about how we handle Code of Conduct reports.

### Emergency Services

**_For Emergency Services at the event, please dial (112)._**

### Social Media and Conference Updates

Subscribe to our [Slack Channel][7] #CHAOSScon for updates about the conference and to coordinate meetups. 

Follow us on [Linked In][8] for more information during the conference. We encourage you to use the hashtag #chaosscon if you are posting about our conference on social media. 

### Diversity Access Tickets and Family Friendliness

Please email Elizabeth Barron <elizabeth@chaoss.community> for more information on how to get a Diversity Access Ticket. Diversity Access Tickets have graciously been donated by our friends at [OLS][9].

CHAOSScon is a family-friendly event, and children under 18 are welcome to attend.
