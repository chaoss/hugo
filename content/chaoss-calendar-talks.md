## <span style="color: #05b59f;">CHAOSS Calendar of Upcoming Talks</span>

### Want to connect with a CHAOTIC? Find us here!

If you would like to see where our CHAOTICs are in the wild, this is a calendar of upcoming talks at events. If you&#8217;re a CHAOTIC and you would like to add your own talk, here&#8217;s how you can do that:

  1. On the calendar, click on the day of your talk
  2. A pop-up will appear, where you can enter your: 
      1. Talk title
      2. Your name
      3. Link to the description of your talk on the website of the event
  3. Click save and your event is added!
