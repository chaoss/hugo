## <span style="color: #05b59f;">CHAOSS Calendar</span>

### Join us for CHAOSS Community and **Working Group** video conferences

Because transparency is one of our core values, we record and publish all community and Working Group calls to [CHAOSStube][1] (our YouTube channel). This should not deter you from participating if you would prefer to keep your information private! Here are a few adjustments you can make to safely join the calls:

  * Keep your camera turned off during the call
  * Make sure your real name isn&#8217;t shown in your Zoom settings
  * Instead of speaking, use Zoom&#8217;s chat functionality to communicate (we heavily integrate chats into the flow of voice conversation)
