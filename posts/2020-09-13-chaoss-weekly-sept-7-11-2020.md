<p dir="ltr">
  In case you missed it, we released a video for anyone who wants a quick summary of the new metrics and changes with our latest August 2020 release. You can <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4759JrmljtU">watch on Youtube</a>, or right here: [embedyt] https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4759JrmljtU[/embedyt]We are considering producing more videos like these, so if there&#8217;s anything you think would be a great topic for a video, let us know!

### <span style="color: #980000">CHAOSScast Episode 17: Antonio Nardella from IOTA</span>

This week we hosted Antonio Nardella for a lively conversation about community management at IOTA, what metrics are most relevant for him, and how he uses those metrics to inform his work. This has been our most popular episode to-date, so you definitely won&#8217;t want to miss this one. Listen now: https://podcast.chaoss.community/17\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">D&I Badging Program Launching This Week!</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  The D&I Badging team has been working diligently for the past several months to bring this program to life. We couldn&#8217;t be prouder of the work that Matt Snell, Aastha Bist and Xiaoya Xia have done in building this program from scratch. Special thanks to Saleh Motaal, and all those who participated in the pilot testing and who provided feedback. This was truly a team effort, and we wish the whole team the best of luck.
</p>
