### <span style="color: #980000">Meeting Cancellations for OSSNA Next Week</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  Because there are a lot of CHAOSS members also participating in Open Source Summit North America (OSSNA) in some capacity, some of the Working Groups have cancelled their regular meetings next week. It&#8217;s a good idea to double-check the agendas and <a href="https://chaoss.local/participate/">calendar</a> for the meetings you usually attend. Or, if you show up next week and no one else does, you&#8217;ll know why. 🙂
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #980000">Latest CHAOSScast with Chris Aniszczyk</span>

<p dir="ltr">
  Episode #6 of CHAOSScast has arrived! This week our guest was Chris Aniszczyk from CNCF and TODO. We discussed his role in the fastest growing Open Source Foundation ever and the vital role community health plays. We also talked about diversity and inclusion, DevStats, Percy Jackson, Code Together, and <em>13th</em>. Listen here: <a href="https://podcast.chaoss.community/6">https://podcast.chaoss.community/6</a>
</p>
