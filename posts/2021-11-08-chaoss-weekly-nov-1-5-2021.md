### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder &#8211; Double-Check Meeting Times</span>

As the US changed clocks for Daylight Savings Time over the weekend, we have caught up with most of the rest of the world. And because all **CHAOSS meetings align with the US Central/Chicago time zone,** it might be wise to double-check when the meetings are happening for your location.

You can [join the CHAOSS Slack channel][1] and the meeting reminders posted in the #general channel will automatically adjust themselves to your time zone, or you can use a date-time conversion tool like [this one][2].

Hope to see you soon!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">DEI Badging 3.0 Released!</span>

Congratulations to the DEI Badging Team for releasing the latest version of our DEI Badging Initiative for events. The goals for this release were to add new metrics to the checklist, improve the reviewer guidelines, and update the README for clarity. The release met those goals and included the following changes:

<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="2">
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">DEI badging repo folded into event-diversity-and-inclusion</span>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="2">
  Combined Attendee and Speaker Demographics into one metric: Event Demographics
</li>
<li aria-level="2">
  Added Inclusive Experience at Event, and Time Inclusion for Virtual Events to the checklist
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;" aria-level="2">
  Clarified documentation in multiple places
</li>
<li aria-level="2">
  Added more reviewers
</li>

The DEI Badging Initiative passed its one year mark and continues to make a huge impact on the open source event ecosystem, as it has now badged [42 events!][3] \[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">CHAOSScast Episode #47 &#8211; Building the GrimoireLab Tutorial through OSPP with Veerasamy Sevagen</span>

This week we had the pleasure of hosting our student that participated in the [Summer 2021 OSPP (Open Source Promotion Plan).][4] This was a new mentorship program for CHAOSS and we were fortunate to have Veerasamy Sevagen join us. Listen to hear more about the program, the GrimoireLab tutorial Sevagen built and, his experience with being a part of the CHAOSS community. [https://podcast.chaoss.community/47.][5]\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
