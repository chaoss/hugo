### <span style="color: #008080;">Happy New Year! We are Back</span>

CHAOSS meetings have resumed in earnest, so if you are interested in connecting with the CHAOSS community, we are back in action! As a reminder, you can see the full calendar of all our meetings on the [CHAOSS website][1], and you can also [join us on Slack.][2]

### <span style="color: #008080;">CHAOSScon EU 2025 exciting updates</span>

We have exciting news to share regarding CHAOScon EU 2025!

  * Our [schedule is confirmed, ][3]including our afternoon keynote, Dr. Katherine Skinner! Dr. Katherine Skinner is the Director of Programs at Invest in Open Infrastructure, and she will be speaking about how to evaluate the ways of assessing community health of open infrastructure.
  * Our schedule includes afternoon breakout sessions for CHAOSS Software (Augur and GrimoireLab), an unconference experience, and thanks to our friends at [CURIOSS][4], a special Academic OSS Breakout session. There will be something for everyone!
  * After the conference, there will be a social event, which is open to everyone. We will have a special family-friendly activity available for everyone! Thank you to our sponsor, [ING][5] for making this possible.
  * Thanks to our friends at [OLS][6], we have Diversity Access Tickets available. Please reach out to Elizabeth Barron if you are interested in using one of these tickets.
  * Thank you to our other wonderful Gold and Bronze sponsors who are helping make this event possible: [Red Hat][7], [Google][8], and [Bitergia][9]

Read more about registration and sponsorship opportunities on the CHAOSScon EU 2025 page of our website: [https://chaoss.local/chaosscon-2025-eu/.][3]

### <span style="color: #008080;">New on the CHAOSS Blog: What Happens to Relicensed Open Source Projects and Their Forks</span>

[In the latest post on the CHAOSS blog][10], Dawn Foster shares research into what happens to open source projects and their forks when it undergoes a license change.  She provides data-driven insight into several projects that have gone through this process, and what the implications might mean. This is a fascinating look into the relicensing impact on open source projects and communities, so you won&#8217;t want to miss this one.

### <span style="color: #008080;">Community Reminders: </span>

#### <span style="color: #008080;">Add your Community Contributions via an Issue on GitHub</span>

We now have an easier way to add your community contribution to our [community-contributions.md][16] document. Simply open an issue using the [CHAOSS Contribution Issue Template][17], and when you submit your issue, your contribution will be added.

#### <span style="color: #008080;">Let the Comms Team Help You Spread Your News!</span>

Are you giving a talk at an upcoming conference? Have you appeared on a recent podcast or been a guest contributor to a blog? Is your working group looking for volunteers for a project? If you have something CHAOSS related and you&#8217;d like to share it with the CHAOSS community (both internally and externally) then let the Communications Working Group know! We will be happy to share things on the official CHAOSS channels. Simply login to Slack and then go to the [Comms Team Submission Form][18] (or type the command _/comms team submission form_) to let us know what you&#8217;re working on.

#### <span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen-Sans, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif; color: #008080;">Looking for Podcast Questions and Ideas for CHAOSScast</span>

Alice Sowerby and the CHAOSScast team would love to hear your questions, comments, and ideas for new episodes of CHAOSScast! Feel free to send an email to **podcast@chaoss.community** with your thoughts.

#### <span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen-Sans, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif; color: #008080;">CHAOTIC of the Week Nomination Form</span>

Have you noticed a fellow CHAOTIC doing something amazing for CHAOSS? Has there been someone who is going above and beyond? If you&#8217;d like to nominate them for CHAOTIC of the Week, now you can! With our CHAOTIC of the Week feature, we like to celebrate their contributions to CHAOSS and enable our whole community to get to know them a little better. It&#8217;s a great way to show appreciation for our fellow CHAOTICs so don&#8217;t hesitate to nominate someone [here][19].
