### <span style="color: #993300;">We are Chaotics! </span>

After much polling, the community decided to name ourselves **Chaotics**. Hooray! If you want to see the poll you can see the results here: <https://voteupapp.com/shared/lCHNiOfnO>. Thanks to everyone who participated!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Changes to Asia-Pacific Community & Risk Working Groups</span>

In case you missed it, here are two changes to be aware of:

  * NO Asia-Pacific Community Meeting this week (Happy New Year!)
  * Risk Working is meeting this Thursday, February 10 at 1:00 pm US Central/Chicago time (The bi-weekly meeting weeks shifted.)

As always, we meet at https://zoom.us/my/chaoss and you can always find an updated calendar of meetings on our [Participate][1] page.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Office Hours are Back</span>

This is a reminder that our open Office Hours for newcomers (or anyone, really) happen **every Tuesday from 9:00 am &#8211; 10:00 am US Central/Chicago** **time**. Feel free to join as you wish at any time during that hour at <https://zoom.us/my/chaoss>. No registration is needed and you can ask anything you want about the CHAOSS project!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]

### <span style="color: #993300;">Reminder: Send Us Your Photos before Feb 15</span>

Because the community has grown so much over the last 5 years, we thought it would be fun to put together a photo mural of all our community members, past and present. If you&#8217;ve attended a meeting, joined our Slack, been a guest on our podcast, helped develop a metric, opened an issue, or simply read one of these gorgeous newsletters, then you are part of our community! You can see the specifics of what we&#8217;re looking for and where you can upload your photos here: <https://lists.linuxfoundation.org/pipermail/chaoss/2022-January/002607.html>. Please join the fun!\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_column\_text\]
