### CHAOSS at FOSSY in Portland, OR {.has-vivid-red-color.has-text-color.wp-block-heading}

Next week, CHAOSS will be at [FOSSY][1] in Portland, Oregon with our very first booth! If you&#8217;ll be at that conference, you should definitely stop by and get a CHAOSS sticker! You can also sign up to win a raffle by answering a few questions about how you think about metrics in your open source project. We&#8217;re giving away an awesome LEGO Globe so make sure to stop by!  
We also have quite a few CHAOTICS who will be giving presentations at the conference. These talks won&#8217;t be recorded so you can only catch them in person!

### CHAOSS on Instagram {.has-vivid-red-color.has-text-color.wp-block-heading}

CHAOSS now has an Instagram account so make sure to follow for some amazing CHAOTIC photography. You can follow us at [@chaossproject][3].

### Next DEI Event Badger Orientation July 11 {.has-vivid-red-color.has-text-color.wp-block-heading}

If you are interested in becoming a new Badger for our DEI Event Badging program, there is an orientation scheduled for **July 11, 2023 at 12:00 US Central / Chicago time** (right after the Weekly Community Call). You don’t have to register for this orientation and you can simply show up. If you’d like to be included on the invitation, so that it’s added to your personal calendar, just let Elizabeth Barron know or fill out our <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScXE5LC8n7o5lkCNLh22b3t3PUK8q1pBCCbe6FdBRmgn_nDVA/viewform?usp=sf_link" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">General Interest Form</a>. This is a great way to contribute to the CHAOSS project in a non-coding way, and you don&#8217;t need to have much experience with CHAOSS to begin.
